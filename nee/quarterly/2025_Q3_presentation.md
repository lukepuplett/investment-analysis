## Strategic Initiatives

### Duane Arnold Energy Center Restart
- **Capacity**: 615 MW nuclear facility expected to return to operations by Q1 2029
- **Agreement**: 25-year power purchase agreement with Google
- **Purpose**: Power Google's growing cloud and AI infrastructure in Iowa
- **Economic Impact**: Creates thousands of jobs and delivers more than $9 billion in economic benefits to Iowa
- **EPS Contribution**: Expected to contribute up to $0.16 of annual adjusted EPS on average over its first 10 years of operation
- **Advanced Nuclear**: Agreement to explore development of advanced nuclear generation
- **Google Partnership**: NextEra Energy and Google have executed over 3.5 GW of energy projects across America

### Tax Credit Phase-Out Timeline
- **Wind**: Eligible through 2030
- **Solar**: Eligible through 2030
- **Storage**: Eligible through 2039 (100% in 2037, 75% in 2038, 50% in 2039)
- **Nuclear**: Eligible through 2039 (100% in 2037, 75% in 2038, 50% in 2039)
- **Based on**: One Big Beautiful Bill Act (July 2025) and Treasury guidance (August 2025)
