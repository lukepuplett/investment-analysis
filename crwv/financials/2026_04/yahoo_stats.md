## Key Observations & Trends

### Explosive Growth Trajectory
- **Revenue Growth**: 168% YoY in FY2025 ($5.1B vs $1.9B in 2024), with Q4 up 110% YoY ($1.6B)
- **Fastest to $5B**: CoreWeave is the fastest cloud platform in history to reach $5B in annual revenue
- **Backlog Surge**: $66.8B in contracted revenue backlog represents 13x current annual revenue and 3.1 GW of contracted power capacity waiting to be built and monetized

### Capacity Expansion Accelerating
- **Q4 Deployment**: Added 260 MW in Q4 alone across 43 active data centers (up from 32 at start of 2025)
- **2026 Guidance**: Target 5+ GW of additional capacity beyond already contracted footprint by 2030
- **CapEx Intensity**: 2026 CapEx guidance of $30B+ (2.6x revenue guidance implies continuing heavy investment in growth)

### Profitability & Margins Currently Compressed
- **GAAP Loss**: ($4.2B) net loss TTM driven by massive CapEx investments
- **Management Guidance**: 140% revenue growth projected in 2026; long-term margin targets of 25%-30% (currently deeply negative due to infrastructure build-out)
- **Business Model**: Deliberately investing ahead of revenue to fulfill contracted demand; CapEx acceleration in Q4 viewed as "high-quality" revenue capacity for 2026

### Customer Diversification & Monetization
- **Customers >$1M Annual**: Grew ~150% YoY, including new AI natives (Cognition, Cursor, Mercado Libre, Midjourney, Runway) plus hyperscalers
- **Platform Cross-Sell**: ~80% of customers >$1M annual have adopted storage products; Weights & Biases partnership generating hundreds of millions in annual contract value (ACV)
- **Software Monetization**: New revenue stream emerging from proprietary cloud stack (SKI, Mission Control) with NVIDIA partnership for licensing beyond CoreWeave data centers

### Market Position
- **Competitive Scale**: Larger than 15 largest NeoClouds across North America/Europe combined (per third-party estimates)
- **Execution Awards**: First cloud to reach NVIDIA Exemplar Cloud status for GB200; SemiAnalysis' sole Platinum-ranked AI cloud
- **Technology Leadership**: Leading edge on GPU deployments (Grace Blackwell at scale); positioning for Rubin, Vera CPU, and BlueField in 2H 2026

### Valuation Context
- **P/S Multiple**: 6.24x on ~$5.1B TTM revenue (premium for AI infrastructure but not extreme given 168% growth and backlog)
- **Price Target**: Consensus $123.50 (current $118.25), implying modest upside (4.4%)
- **Pre-Profitability**: Heavy loss TTM due to CapEx; path to profitability dependent on margin expansion as revenue scales
