# CoreWeave (CRWV) – Financial Analysis (April 15, 2026)

## Executive Summary

**BLUF: CoreWeave delivered exceptional $5.1B FY2025 revenue (+168% YoY) with 81% Q4 gross margins and a $66.8B contracted backlog, but remains in heavy investment mode with ($4.2B) net losses and $30B+ 2026 CapEx guidance; profitability achievable in 2-3 years if 25%-30% margin targets hold and CapEx moderates post-capacity build.**

## Key Financial Takeaways

1. **Revenue Momentum Exceptional**: 168% growth rare for $5B+ scale company; backlog of 13x revenue provides rare multi-year visibility
2. **Margin Profile Improving**: Q4 gross margins (81%) reflect manufacturing efficiency and favorable mix; path to 25%-30% operating margins achievable by 2027-2028
3. **CapEx Intensity Peak**: 2026 CapEx (2.6x revenue) represents investment peak; moderation expected post-2026 as key capacity targets filled
4. **Cash Flow Bifurcated**: Strong operating cash flow (~$2.5B–$3.0B) more than offset by CapEx, resulting in deeply negative FCF; financing reliance critical
5. **Profitability Timing**: Realistic inflection point 2027-2028 as CapEx moderates and backlog monetizes; investors must tolerate 2+ years of losses
