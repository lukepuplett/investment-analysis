# CoreWeave, Inc. (CRWV) – Growth Analysis

**Date:** June 2025  
**Analyst:** Investment Analysis Repository  
**Most Recent Period:** Q1 2025 (Ended March 31, 2025)

## Executive Summary

**BLUF: CoreWeave demonstrates **exceptional hypergrowth** with TTM revenue of $2.71B showing 420% YoY growth, driven by explosive AI infrastructure demand. The company has a massive $25.9B revenue backlog providing strong visibility, with infrastructure capacity growing from 420 MW active to 1.6 GW contracted. Growth is **infrastructure-intensive** with high capital requirements (-$7.60B TTM free cash flow) but exceptional unit economics (74.28% gross margin, 62% adjusted EBITDA margin). Key growth catalysts include AI demand acceleration, strategic partnerships (OpenAI, IBM, NVIDIA), capacity expansion, and technology leadership. **Recommendation: HIGH RISK/HIGH REWARD** - Exceptional growth opportunity but requires significant capital investment and execution excellence.**
