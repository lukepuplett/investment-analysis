## Stress Test: Key Assumptions Simultaneously Adverse

**Scenario: 3 Worst Assumptions Hit Simultaneously**
- Revenue growth slows to 12% (miss guidance, first orders falter)
- Operating margin stalls at 12% (OpEx discipline lost, SaaS mix issues)
- DBNR compresses to 105% (NRR crisis from price-sensitive churn)
- WACC rises to 11% (risk premium rises as execution questions mount)
- Terminal growth declines to 2% (market questions long-term viability)

**Result:** Price target **$18-25** — approximately 25-40% downside from current $25
