## Thesis Coherence Assessment

### Pillar Alignment Check

**Profitability Inflection ↔ Operating Leverage Emerging ↔ Fortress Balance Sheet**
- ✅ **Coherent:** Profitability path is driven by operating leverage on stable business with fortress balance sheet providing cushion for investments
- Implication: Company can invest in growth while approaching profitability

**First Orders Inflection ↔ Sales Capacity Ramp ↔ Net New ARR Growth**
- ✅ **Coherent:** First order inflection validates core business health; sales ramp amplifies this with new capacity; net new ARR should accelerate
- Implication: Bookings growth inflection is achievable if ramps succeed

**Enterprise Expansion ↔ Price-Sensitive Stabilization ↔ NRR Stabilization**
- ⚠️ **Partially Coherent:** Enterprise expansion is happening (100K +18%, $1M +26%), BUT price-sensitive cohort stabilization is unproven
- Implication: NRR will stabilize (not decline further) only if both enterprise expansion AND price-sensitive stabilization both validate
- Risk: If price-sensitive stabilization fails, NRR compression extends and offset enterprise gains

**DAP Launch ↔ AI Orchestration Positioning ↔ New TAM**
- ⚠️ **Partially Coherent:** DAP is strategically positioned correctly and early customer feedback is strong, BUT timeline risk is real (70% self-managed, 6-month upgrade curve)
- Implication: FY27 contribution minimal (as guided); FY28+ is material upside opportunity
- Risk: If adoption is slower than expected, upside gets pushed further out

**Valuation Repricing ↔ Multiple Expansion Potential**
- ✅ **Coherent:** Stock repriced down from growth stock (16x) to near-profitability valuation (4.2x); if profitability inflects, stock re-rates to 8-10x, creating +100% upside
- Implication: Asymmetric risk/reward (40% downside vs 100%+ upside) is attractive at current price
