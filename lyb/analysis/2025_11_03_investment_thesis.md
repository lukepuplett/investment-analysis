## Core Investment Thesis

### Primary Thesis: Cyclical Recovery Play with Structural Advantages

LyondellBasell is a cyclical recovery play with strong structural advantages that position it well for value creation as markets normalize. The company's cost-advantaged positioning in North America and Middle East, proprietary technology leadership, and strategic portfolio repositioning create a compelling investment case at current trough valuations.

### Key Investment Pillars

1. **Deep Cyclical Trough Valuation:** Trading at 0.41x Price/Sales, 12.99x Forward P/E
2. **Structural Cost Advantages:** 70%+ capacity share in cost-advantaged regions expected
3. **Accelerating Capacity Rationalization:** ~30M tons of closures improving market balances
4. **Strong Financial Position:** $6.5B liquidity, investment-grade rating maintained
5. **Technology Leadership:** Proprietary technologies in polyolefins and circular economy
6. **Strategic Repositioning:** Portfolio transformation improving competitive position

### 3. Accelerating Capacity Rationalization

**Global Rationalization:**
- **Total Closures:** ~30M+ tons through 2028 (~10% of global supply)
- **China:** ~21.3M tons expected rationalization
- **Europe:** ~3.5M tons anticipated closures
- **Japan/South Korea:** ~5.4-7.4M tons announced closures

**Market Impact:**
- Improving supply/demand balances globally
- Reducing competitive pressure from oversupply
- Supporting pricing recovery as markets normalize
- Creating more constructive mid-term outlook

**Timing:**
- 30% of closures announced in past 12 months
- Acceleration of rationalization across regions
- Domino effect helping offset new supply
- Expected to take several years to fully impact

### 4. Strong Financial Position

**Liquidity and Balance Sheet:**
- **Total Liquidity:** $6.5B providing significant buffer
- **Cash Position:** $1.8B cash and cash equivalents
- **Investment-Grade:** Credit rating maintained
- **Debt Management:** Manageable debt maturity profile

**Cash Generation:**
- **Cash Conversion:** 99% TTM, 135% in Q3 2025
- **Operating Cash Flow:** $2.4B TTM
- **Free Cash Flow:** $463M TTM (down from peak but positive)
- **Cash Improvement Plan:** $600M incremental cash flow target

**Capital Allocation:**

- **Share Buybacks:** Minimal in 2025-2026, preserving cash
- **CAPEX:** Reduced to $1.2B in 2026 from $1.7B in 2025
- **Discipline:** Balanced approach preserving flexibility
