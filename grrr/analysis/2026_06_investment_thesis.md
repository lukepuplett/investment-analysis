## Bull Case (Conviction: 8/10)

### Narrative
GRRR is the only credible provider of vertically integrated, sovereign AI infrastructure in Asia. $5B+ backlog is real, signed, and executable on current timeline. Operating cash flow inflection (Q1 +$6.6M) validates execution. Fortress balance sheet ($98.9M cash) + non-recourse project financing de-risks capex.

**Revenue path:**
- 2026E: $180M (midpoint) = +78% YoY
- 2027E: $500M+ = +178% growth (driven by Yotta full ramp, Korat online, co-location scale)
- 2028E: $1B+ (if 500-MW capacity by end 2027 is achieved)

**Margin expansion:**
- 2026E: Blended 30–40% gross margin → EBITDA 25–30%
- 2027E: Blended 55–65% gross margin → EBITDA 35–40%
- 2028E: 60–70% gross margin → EBITDA 40%+ (mature scale)

**Valuation at $500M revenue:**
- If 30% EBITDA margin = $150M EBITDA
- At 8–10x EBITDA (infrastructure comparison: Equinix 20x, but GRRR smaller + higher growth) = $1.2–1.5B valuation
- Current market cap: $585M → 2–2.5x upside over 18–24 months

**Key drivers:**
1. Yotta GPU ramp (Sep–Nov 2026 revenue recognition)
2. Korat construction begins; establishes owned asset base (reduces competitive threats)
3. Project financing closes; de-risks capex
4. EBITDA positive; inflection for profitability narrative
5. Customer wins beyond Yotta (Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore mentioned in call)

### Edge Cases (Upside Scenarios)
- **Korat capacity expanded faster** (government backing accelerates timeline) → $1B revenue possible in 2027 vs. $500M plan
- **Vera Rubin (new GPU) launch delayed** → GRRR's B300 allocation becomes more valuable; pricing power increases
- **Equinix acquisition of competitor fails** → GRRR moat uncontested for 3+ years
- **M&A approach from strategic buyer** (hyperscaler, infrastructure fund) → liquidity event at 2.5–3x current price

Organizational execution at 5–10x scale is the key variable; if successful, $500M revenue + 35% EBITDA margin by 2027 is achievable, supporting 2–2.5x valuation upside.

**Best case for investors:** Q3/Q4 2026 execution validation (Yotta online, financing closed, margins expanding) drives 50–80% stock appreciation by end 2026. Long-term (2027–2028), successful capacity scale-up (500 MW by 2027) supports $1B+ valuation.

**Worst case:** Execution stumbles in H2 2026; stock re-rates down 30–45%. Base case: Conservative execution; 1.8x valuation upside over 18–24 months. 

**Recommendation for growth-focused, emerging market investors:** **BUY. Target allocation 3–5%. Hold through 2027 profitability inflection.**
