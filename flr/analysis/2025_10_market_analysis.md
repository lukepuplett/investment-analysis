## End Market Analysis

### Infrastructure Market

**Market Dynamics:**
- **Size:** Trillion-dollar global infrastructure market with strong U.S. investment themes
- **Growth Drivers:** Infrastructure bills, aging infrastructure replacement, urban development
- **Fluor Position:** Strong contractor in transportation, transit, and public works projects

**Recent Performance:**
- Q2 2025 backlog: $20.6B in Urban Solutions (includes infrastructure projects)
- Key projects: Gordie Howe Bridge (97% complete), CTA Red/Purple Line (operational), I-35 Phase 2 (58% complete)
- Challenges: Three infrastructure projects experiencing cost growth ($54M impact)

**Market Outlook:**
- **Near-term:** Client hesitancy due to trade uncertainty and cost escalation
- **Medium-term:** Infrastructure investment acceleration expected once trade policy stabilizes
- **Long-term:** Strong secular tailwinds from infrastructure investment bills and urban development

### Mining & Metals Market

- **Growth Drivers:** Energy transition (copper, lithium, rare earths), reshoring critical minerals, commodity demand
- **Fluor Position:** Leading EPC contractor with relationships with major miners (BHP, Rio Tinto, Freeport-McMoRan)

**Market Outlook:**

- **Long-term:** Exceptional growth potential from energy transition demand (copper, lithium, rare earths)
- **U.S. Opportunity:** Significant potential from reshoring critical minerals and domestic mining development
