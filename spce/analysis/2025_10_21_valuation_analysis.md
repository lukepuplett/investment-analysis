## Executive Summary

**BLUF: Virgin Galactic is severely undervalued with $1.11B in assets vs $233M market cap (79% discount), but faces high execution risk. Sum-of-parts suggests $19.30/share vs current $3.91. Management projects $450M-$1B revenue potential, but success depends on 2026 commercial launch. High-risk, high-reward investment with significant asset backing.**

Virgin Galactic presents a challenging valuation scenario as a pre-revenue company with significant execution risks but substantial upside potential. Current market valuation of $232.67M reflects the high-risk nature of the investment, trading at 0.88x book value and 82.95x price-to-sales (TTM). Multiple valuation approaches suggest fair value range of $2.50-$8.00 per share, with significant upside potential if commercial operations succeed but substantial downside risk if execution fails.
