## Key Observations & Red Flags

### Revenue Trajectory
- **Collapse from peak:** Revenue down 54% from FY2022 ($1.5B) to TTM ($687.6M)
- **Recent stabilization:** FY2025 revenue ($654M) relatively flat to TTM
- **FY2023-2024 decline:** Loss of $88M in revenue suggests significant market downturn

### Profitability Breakdown ⚠️
- **Operating loss expansion:** From -$48M (FY2024) to -$39M (FY2025) to -$32M (TTM)
- **Margin compression:** Gross margin compressed from 49.8% (FY2022) to 42.2% (TTM)
- **Net loss TTM:** Despite minimal FY2025 earnings ($213k), TTM shows -$64.6M loss
- **Cost structure not adjusting:** OpEx (as % of revenue) has grown significantly

### Unusual Items Impact 🔴
- FY2025 benefited from $75.987M gain on business sale, masking underlying losses
- Repeated impairment charges (FY2024: $44.5M, FY2023: $21.5M) signal potential asset quality issues
- Normalized EBITDA TTM ($6.9M) dramatically worse than reported figures

### EBITDA Volatility
- TTM EBITDA negative at -$32.9M, a stark contrast to FY2022 ($498.5M)
- Normalized EBITDA only $6.9M TTM—minimal cushion
- High depreciation ($17M TTM) suggests capital-intensive business

### Positive Elements
- Interest income of $22M provides some profitability cushion
- Share count reduction (60.2M to 52.6M) represents ~13% shrinkage
- Tax provision relatively low given losses (deferred tax benefit likely)
