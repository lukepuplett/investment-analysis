### Red Flags 🔴

1. **Unsustainable Capital Returns:** Company returned $120.6M (dividends + buybacks) on only $76M FCF TTM
2. **Dividend Payout Ratio:** 70% of FCF to dividends despite operating losses; dividend grew YoY
3. **Aggressive Buybacks During Downturn:** $150.8M repurchased in FY2024 despite losses
4. **Cash Depletion Accelerating:** Down $275M from FY2022 peak; burning at -$44.5M/yr after capital returns
5. **Working Capital Volatility:** Swings from -$79M to +$42M suggest operational instability
6. **Investment Liquidation:** Selling down short-term investments to fund operations/dividends

### Positive Elements ✅

1. **Still Generating Operating Cash:** $85.7M OCF TTM despite -$64.6M net loss
2. **Minimal Capex:** Only $9.7M spend provides flexibility
3. **Debt-Free:** No meaningful debt service required
4. **Non-Cash Charges:** $39.8M impairments help OCF relative to net income
