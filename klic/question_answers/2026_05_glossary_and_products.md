## Semiconductor Industry Jargon & Acronyms

### Memory Technologies

| Acronym | Full Name | Definition | KLIC Role |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **HBM** | High-Bandwidth Memory | Advanced memory stacks that provide ultra-high data throughput for AI accelerators; KLIC's primary TCB target | **Primary growth driver FY26-27** |
| **NAND** | NOT-AND Flash Memory | Non-volatile storage technology; used in SSDs, USB drives; KLIC's legacy ball bonding application | **Established revenue base** |
| **DRAM** | Dynamic Random-Access Memory | Volatile memory used for processing; Vertical Wire targets this segment | **Emerging opportunity** |
| **HBF** | High-Bandwidth Flash | Emerging memory technology combining NAND flash capacity with HBM-class bandwidth; KLIC exploring TCB assembly | **CY2027 opportunity** |
| **Stack DRAM** | Stacked DRAM | Multiple DRAM dies stacked using die-stacking technology (Vertical Wire); cost-effective alternative to HBM | **Vertical Wire target app** |

### Packaging & Assembly Technologies

| Acronym | Full Name | Definition | KLIC Role |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **TCB** | Thermo-Compression Bonding | Direct metal-to-metal bonding using heat and pressure; KLIC's advanced packaging flagship | **>$100M FY26 revenue target** |
| **Fluxless TCB** | Flux-free Thermo-Compression | TCB without chemical flux; KLIC's proprietary process reducing defects | **Competitive differentiator** |
| **Hybrid Bonding** | Copper-to-Copper Bonding | Alternative technology merging metals directly; TSMC's preferred HBM approach; competitive threat | **Primary competitive risk** |
| **Ball Bonding** | Wire Ball Bonding | Gold/copper ball formed on wire, ultrasonically bonded to pads; KLIC's core legacy product | **$400-500M annual revenue** |
| **Wedge Bonding** | Wire Wedge Bonding | Alternative wire bonding approach (competitor Palomar specialty); KLIC minimal presence | **N/A** |
| **Flip-Chip** | Flip-Chip Interconnect | Direct die-to-substrate bonding; component of advanced packaging | **Emerging KLIC opportunity** |
| **TSV** | Through-Silicon Via | Vertical copper connections through silicon; used in 3D stacking | **Not KLIC focus** |
| **Underfill** | Encapsulation Material | Adhesive filled under flip-chip devices for reliability | **KLIC dispense tools apply this** |
| **Vertical Wire** | Vertical Die Stacking | KLIC-proprietary technology for cost-effective DRAM stacking | **$80-120M FY2027 opportunity** |

### Logic & Chip Architecture

| Acronym | Full Name | Definition | KLIC Role |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **AI/ML** | Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning | AI workloads driving demand for HBM and advanced packaging | **Primary demand driver** |
| **Chiplet** | Multi-Die Logic | Smaller dies combined to create complex systems; requires advanced packaging (TCB) | **Major KLIC opportunity** |
| **Heterogeneous Logic** | Mixed Die Types | Combining different process nodes/die types; chiplets require TCB for interconnect | **KLIC competitive strength** |
| **Nodes (5nm, 3nm, N2)** | Process Technology | Smaller = more advanced; TSMC N5/N3/N2 are leading-edge targets for KLIC tools | **Primary customer base** |
| **ADAS** | Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems | Autonomous vehicle sensors/processing; driver of automotive semiconductor content | **10-year growth story** |
| **EV** | Electric Vehicle | Battery electric vehicles; driving power semiconductor content growth | **Long-term KLIC opportunity** |

### Manufacturing & Industry

| Acronym | Full Name | Definition | KLIC Role |
|---------|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Fab** | Semiconductor Fabrication Plant | Wafer manufacturing facility | **KLIC customer** |
| **Foundry** | Contract Chip Manufacturer | TSMC, Samsung, SMIC (designs chips for others) | **Primary KLIC customer type** |
| **IDM** | Integrated Device Manufacturer | Designs and manufactures own chips (Intel, NVIDIA); increasingly outsourcing to foundries | **KLIC customer** |
| **OSAT** | Outsourced Semiconductor Assembly & Test | ASE, Amkor, Nantong (assembly/packaging specialists) | **Primary KLIC customer segment** |
| **CIW** | Customer Interaction Wafer | Test vehicle for process validation | **Not KLIC focus** |

## KLIC Product Portfolio

### Core Products (Revenue Generating)

#### 1. **Ball Bonding Systems** — LEGACY/CORE
- **Description:** Ultrasonic wire bonding equipment using gold/copper balls
- **Applications:** General semiconductor assembly (data center logic, memory, automotive, consumer)
- **Revenue Estimate:** ~$400-500M annually (est. 60% of total)
- **Market Position:** Established leader; commoditizing but still differentiated in high-performance segment
- **Growth Rate:** Flat-to-declining long-term; growing with data center capex near-term

#### 2. **Thermo-Compression Bonding (TCB) Systems** — ADVANCED/GROWTH
- **Description:** Direct metal-to-metal bonding equipment (fluxless KLIC specialty)
- **Applications:** HBM assembly, chiplet interconnect, advanced logic packaging
- **Product Lines:**
  - **Fluxless TCB (KLIC proprietary):** Best-in-class alternative to hybrid bonding
  - **Standard TCB:** Legacy TCB approach
- **Revenue Estimate:** >$100M FY2026 target (growing from ~$50-60M baseline)
- **Market Position:** First-mover in fluxless TCB; 120 systems in field; 50% fluxless adoption
- **Growth Rate:** 20-30% annually; 2-3x market growth

#### 3. **Advanced Dispense Systems** — EMERGING
- **Product:** Echelon dispense system (launched November 2025)
- **Applications:** Underfill, mold compound, adhesive dispensing for advanced packaging
- **Revenue Estimate:** <$15M FY2026 (nascent stage)
- **Market Position:** New entrant to established market (Nordson, Musashi dominant)
- **Growth Rate:** Potential; customer qualification phase

#### 4. **Vertical Wire Systems** — EMERGING/PROPRIETARY
- **Description:** Vertical die stacking for cost-effective DRAM bandwidth
- **Applications:** Low-power DRAM, mobile processors, edge computing (AI accelerators)
- **Revenue Estimate:** $0-5M FY2026 (early adoption); $80-120M FY2027+ (if validated)
- **Market Position:** KLIC pioneer; 8 customers engaged with positive feedback
- **Growth Rate:** 30%+ if adoption accelerates

#### 5. **Aftermarket Services** — SUPPORTING
- **Description:** Maintenance, parts, support for installed equipment base
- **Revenue Estimate:** ~$50-60M annually (6-9% of total)
- **Growth Rate:** Growing with utilization improvements (+14% YoY Q1 FY26)

**Status:**
