Earnings call

Kulicke & Soffa delivered Q1 FY26 revenue and earnings above expectations, driven by a 27% sequential and 90% YoY increase in general semiconductor revenue and strong demand in memory and advanced packaging. Management guided for a 15% sequential revenue increase to $230M for Q2, with gross margins stable at 49%, and expects the second half of FY26 to be 15%-20% higher than the first half, citing robust utilization and ongoing strength in advanced packaging and power semiconductor segments.
