# Robinhood Markets, Inc. (HOOD)

## Company Overview

Robinhood Markets, Inc. operates a financial-services platform allowing retail investing, options, crypto trading, debit card and cash-management products, futures, and subscriptions (Robinhood Gold), primarily in the United States.

**Ticker:** HOOD  
**Exchange:** NASDAQ  
**Sector:** Financial Services  
**Industry:** Capital Markets — technology-enabled brokerage  

## Executive Summary (BLUF – Bottom Line Up Front)

**Apr 29, 2026 snapshot:** Robinhood today looks financially strong compared with its loss-making years around 2022–23: trailing revenue is roughly **$4.5B**, margins are unusually high for a brokerage, and filed **Q1 2026** sales grew about **15% year over year**. The tension is **valuation vs. trajectory**: pasted market data implies **premium multiples** (~**37× forward P/E**, **~16× sales**)—the market is priced as if elevated growth and margins will **keep going**. Reality still includes **heavy legal and regulatory baggage**, revenue that moves with trading activity, and **cash flows that swing** with brokerage clearing and customer balances—not a simple quarterly “adjusted earnings” picture. **The stock itself** trades with high **beta (~2.5)**: mood and headlines can overshoot fundamentals. Separate note: charts discussed in coverage are **Robinhood’s share price**, not the **Nasdaq index** ([market analysis](analysis/2026_04_29_market_analysis.md)). Overall read (**not personalized advice**): see [investment thesis](analysis/2026_04_29_investment_thesis.md); treat size and horizon with care until the next full refresh.
