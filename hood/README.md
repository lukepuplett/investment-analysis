# Robinhood Markets, Inc. (HOOD)

## Company Overview

Robinhood Markets, Inc. operates a financial-services platform allowing retail investing, options, crypto trading, debit card and cash-management products, futures, and subscriptions (Robinhood Gold), primarily in the United States.

**Ticker:** HOOD  
**Exchange:** NASDAQ  
**Sector:** Financial Services  
**Industry:** Capital Markets — technology-enabled brokerage
