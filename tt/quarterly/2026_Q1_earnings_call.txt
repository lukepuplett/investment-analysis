Earnings call

Trane Technologies delivered a strong Q1 2026 with organic bookings up 24%, record backlog of $10.7 billion (up 30% vs. year-end 2025), and adjusted EPS growth of 7%. Management raised full-year organic revenue growth guidance to ~7% and adjusted EPS to $14.75-$14.95, citing robust demand in commercial HVAC, especially data centers, and continued services growth, while maintaining a prudent outlook for residential and transport segments.
