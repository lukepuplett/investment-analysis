## BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)

**Trane's valuation reflects premium quality (34.5x trailing P/E, 30.6x forward) justified by 13-15% FY2026 EPS growth and 8-12% long-term organic growth potential. DCF analysis across bull/base/bear scenarios yields intrinsic value of $490-600, with base case $540 implying 15-20% upside. Current 34.5x trailing and 30.6x forward multiples are at the high end of defensible range but not unreasonable for a best-in-class operator in structurally growing markets (data center 30%+ TAM, services double-digit). Valuation is most sensitive to: (1) data center organic growth rate assumption (±10% impact on valuation), (2) long-term ROIC/margin assumptions, (3) terminal growth rate. Risks to upside: execution risks cap growth realization; risks to downside: tariff/macro headwinds accelerate margin compression.**
