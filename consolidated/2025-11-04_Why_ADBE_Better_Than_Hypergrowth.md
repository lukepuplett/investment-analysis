## The "But Growth!" Counterargument

### Why Higher Growth Doesn't Always Win:

1. **Diminishing Returns**: 
   - CRWV's 420% growth comes at the cost of -$7.6B cash burn
   - Each dollar of revenue requires massive capital investment
   - ADBE's 10% growth comes with cash generation

2. **Sustainability**:
   - CRWV's growth rate is unsustainable (no company grows 420% forever)
   - ADBE's growth rate is sustainable and proven over 5+ years
   - When CRWV's growth decelerates, margins may compress

3. **Valuation Risk**:
   - CRWV is priced for perfection (high growth expectations)
   - If growth decelerates, valuation multiple compression
   - ADBE is priced for sustainable growth, less downside risk

4. **Execution Risk**:
   - CRWV must execute perfectly: infrastructure deployment, financing, competition
   - ADBE has proven execution track record
   - One execution mistake at CRWV could be catastrophic
