**Period:** FY2025 (Year Ended 12/31/2025)  
**Date:** May 2026

---

## Executive Summary (BLUF)

**Datadog at $142.85 (May 6, 2026) is trading at 65.5x FY2026E earnings (premium to market), justified by 20%+ revenue growth and credible FY2027 profitability inflection. On a 2.5x PEG ratio basis (FY2027 21.5% EPS growth), valuation is reasonable. Fair value range: $155-180 (limited upside, some downside risk if growth/margin guidance misses). Investment verdict: Fair value at current levels; buy on 10-15% dips ($120-130 range); reduce on 20%+ rallies ($170+). Price target (12-month): $165 (modest 15% upside).**
