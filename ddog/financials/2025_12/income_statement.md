## Key Trends & Observations

- **Revenue Growth:** Strong 27.6% growth from FY2024 to FY2025 ($2.68B → $3.43B)
- **Gross Margin:** Stable at 80.0% in FY2025, slight compression from 80.8% in FY2024
- **Operating Expenses:** Growing faster than revenue (31.7% growth YoY), driven by R&D and Sales/Marketing investments
- **Operating Margin:** Negative at -1.3% in FY2025 vs. +2.0% in FY2024, reflecting increased investment
- **Net Income:** Declined from $183.7M (FY2024) to $107.7M (FY2025), but company remains profitable
- **Net Margin:** Compression from 6.8% to 3.1%, due to operating leverage investments
- **EBITDA:** $193.8M (FY2025), down from $265.9M (FY2024) due to operating expense growth
- **Path to Profitability:** Company is investing heavily in growth (R&D +34%, S&M +26%) while maintaining gross margins
