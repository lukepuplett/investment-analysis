## CASH FLOW QUALITY & TRENDS

### Operating Cash Flow Analysis
- **Strong OCF Growth:** +20.6% YoY to $1.05B, demonstrating sustainable profitability
- **High Conversion:** OCF is 8.5x net income, showing strong cash generation despite lower accounting earnings
- **Working Capital Benefits:** $146M swing in other working capital contributed to OCF growth
- **SaaS Model Strength:** Deferred revenue growth (upfront collections) supports OCF

### Capital Allocation
- **Low CapEx Intensity:** Only 3.9% of revenue, typical for SaaS platforms
- **M&A Activity:** $118M M&A spend in FY2025 (vs $7M in FY2024), indicating strategic acquisitions
- **Investment Rebalancing:** Net $1.08B in short-term investment purchases (treasury management)

### Free Cash Flow Performance
- **Strong FCF Generation:** $915M FCF (26.7% margin) despite $107.7M net income
- **FCF Growth:** +18% YoY, driven by OCF growth partially offset by increased CapEx
- **Quality Metric:** FCF exceeds net income 8.5x, indicating high earnings quality

### Debt Management
- **Debt Reduction Phase:** Repaid $636M debt in FY2025 (vs raised $1.03B in FY2024)
- **Conservative Approach:** Using strong FCF to de-lever while maintaining investment in growth
- **Equity Financing:** $63M from stock option exercises (employee compensation)

### Cash Position Decline
- **Expected Timing:** Cash decreased from $1.25B to $401M due to debt repayment and investing activities
- **Strategic Choice:** Reduced excess cash via debt paydown and investment management
- **Liquidity Remains Strong:** Still have $4.47B in cash + short-term investments when including investments
