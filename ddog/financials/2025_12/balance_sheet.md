## KEY BALANCE SHEET TRENDS & OBSERVATIONS

**Liquidity & Cash Position:**
- Strong liquidity with $4.47B in cash & short-term investments (68% of current assets)
- Working capital increased 24% YoY to $3.79B, indicating strong financial health
- Current ratio of 3.38x is healthy and consistent with SaaS peer norms

**Debt Position:**
- Total debt decreased from $1.84B to $1.28B (down 30% YoY)
- Large current debt payment in FY2024 ($634M) was reduced in FY2025
- Debt/Equity improved significantly from 67.8% to 34.3% — conservative leverage
- Net debt only $582M despite $1.28B total debt, thanks to strong cash position

**Growth & Asset Base:**
- Total assets grew 14.8% YoY to $6.64B, supporting 27.6% revenue growth
- Goodwill & intangibles increased to $545M (from $364M), indicating M&A activity
- PPE growth reflects infrastructure investment for scale

**Deferred Revenue (SaaS Strength):**
- Deferred revenue pool expanded: Current $1.19B (+24% YoY) + Non-Current $69M
- This represents contracted future revenue, providing revenue visibility
- Indicates strong customer commitment and recurring revenue model

**Equity Base:**
- Shareholders' equity grew 37.6% YoY to $3.73B
- Retained earnings turned positive ($138M) after losses in prior years
- Book value per share increased 33.6% to $10.59 from $7.93

**Capital Structure:**
- Conservative balance sheet with strong cash generation supporting growth investments
- Adequate debt capacity for future M&A or strategic initiatives
- Equity funding efficient with capital preserved through deferred revenue model
