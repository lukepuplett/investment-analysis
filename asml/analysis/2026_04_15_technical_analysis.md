## Core Technology: EUV Lithography

### EUV Fundamentals

#### Technology Overview
- **Wavelength:** 13.5 nanometers (extreme ultraviolet)
- **Application:** Advanced semiconductor node patterning (7nm to sub-2nm)
- **Contrast:** Reflection-based optics (absorption at EUV wavelengths)
- **Precision:** Nanometer tolerances required

#### Why EUV is Required
| Challenge | 193nm DUV Limitation | EUV Solution |
|-----------|---------------------|-------------|
| **Resolution** | Diffraction limit ~28nm | EUV: 7nm capable |
| **Multiple Patterning** | 10+ process steps required | EUV: 2-3 steps |
| **Throughput** | Lower due to multiple exposure | EUV: Single exposure |
| **Cost** | More masks, more steps | EUV: Fewer masks, simpler |

**Fundamental Advantage:** EUV enables direct patterning of advanced nodes without multiple masks/exposure/etch cycles.

## Technical Risk Assessment

### Primary Technical Risks
1. **High NA Ecosystem Maturity**
   - **Risk:** Resist/mask technology not ready for 2027 volume ramp
   - **Probability:** Low (ecosystem validation progressing well)
   - **Mitigation:** Early customer collaboration, SPIE success
   - **Impact if occurs:** 1-year delay in High NA ramp

2. **Immersion DUV Recovery Uncertainty**
   - **Risk:** Slower-than-expected recovery in immersion demand
   - **Probability:** Low (Q1 data shows recovery starting)
   - **Mitigation:** Non-EUV guidance indicates growth, not decline
   - **Impact if occurs:** Mix shift toward EUV (higher margins), not revenue risk

3. **1,000W Source Scalability**
   - **Risk:** Source power scaling to 1,000W more challenging than expected
   - **Probability:** Low (demonstration completed)
   - **Mitigation:** Already achieved 1,000W in lab, now moving to production
   - **Impact if occurs:** Throughput targets miss by 10-20%, not fundamental risk

4. **Supply Chain Concentration**
   - **Risk:** Zeiss optics supply disruption
   - **Probability:** Low (exclusive partnership, strategic importance)
   - **Mitigation:** Long-term partnership, both companies invested
   - **Impact if occurs:** Severe (optics critical to EUV), but low likelihood

### Technical Risk Mitigants
- **Diversified product line:** EUV Low NA, High NA, non-EUV all progressing
- **Long development timeline:** Technology roadmap 10+ years visible
- **Customer collaboration:** Early feedback reducing execution risk
- **Proven execution:** Track record of technical delivery
- **R&D investment:** $1B+/year supporting continuous improvement
