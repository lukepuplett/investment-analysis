## Bull Case Deep Dive

### Why AI Capex is Structurally Different

**Historical Semiconductor Cycle Pattern:**
- Boom-bust cycles every 3-5 years
- Peak demand lasts 12-18 months
- Rapid decline over 6-12 months
- Total cycle: 3-4 years from trough to trough

**AI Capex Cycle Differences:**
1. **Structural Demand:** AI infrastructure not discretionary, it's competitive necessity
2. **Long Investment Horizon:** Hyperscalers committed to 2-3 year capex plans
3. **Multiple Layers:** Memory capex (DRAM, HBM, NAND) + logic capex + specialized chips
4. **Diversified Customers:** Not concentrated in 2-3 megacustomers, spread across ecosystem
5. **Government Support:** US/EU subsidies extending capex cycles (CHIPS Act, European Chips Act)

**Implication:** AI capex cycle could extend 5+ years vs. normal 3-4 year cycle

### Customer Backlog & Visibility

**Memory Customers (SK Hynix, Micron, Samsung):**
- Reporting "sold out for 2026"
- Supply constraints extending "beyond 2026"
- Committed to major capacity expansions
- HBM (high-margin) production limited by capacity

**Advanced Logic Customers (TSMC, Samsung Foundry, Intel):**
- Building capacity for "several nodes while ramping 2nm"
- 2nm addressing AI accelerator demand
- Long-term customer commitments supporting orders
- Capacity constraints supporting pricing power

**Implication:** Customer demand visibility extends through 2027+ with confidence.

### Pricing Power Evidence

| Metric | Evidence | Implication |
|--------|----------|------------|
| Gross Margin | 53% in Q1 2026 | Expanding margins despite volume growth |
| Customer Willingness | Zero price resistance | No supplier alternatives |
| Premium Pricing | EUV 3-4x DUV cost | Customers accept premium for no choice |
| Mix Expansion | EUV growing faster | Margin-accretive product mix shift |

### Installed Base as Growth Engine

- Q1 2026: EUR 2.5B installed base revenue (28% of total)
- Growing faster than system sales
- High margin business (estimated 60%+ gross margin)
- Recurring, predictable revenue stream
- Enables customer rapid capacity deployment

**Implication:** Non-system revenue stabilizes through-cycle, reducing volatility.
