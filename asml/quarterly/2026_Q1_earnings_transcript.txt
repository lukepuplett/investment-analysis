Earnings call

ASML delivered Q1 2026 net sales of EUR 8.8 billion, matching guidance, with gross margin at 53%—the high end of expectations—driven by strong installed base revenue and favorable mix. Management raised full-year 2026 revenue guidance to EUR 36-40 billion, citing robust AI-driven demand, capacity expansions, and increased adoption of EUV and immersion lithography. Forward guidance for Q2 2026 projects net sales between EUR 8.4-9 billion and gross margin of 51-52%.
