# Johnson Controls International (JCI) Q3 FY2025 Earnings Presentation

**Date:** July 29, 2025  
**Fiscal Quarter:** Q3 FY2025 (Three Months Ended June 30, 2025)

## Executive Summary

Johnson Controls reported strong Q3 FY2025 results with organic sales growth of 6% and 11% adjusted EPS growth, exceeding guidance. Segment margin expanded 20 basis points to 17.6%. Management raised full-year EPS and free cash flow guidance, while launching a lean business system to drive long-term growth and operational efficiency.

## Key Financial Highlights

### Q3 FY2025 Results
- **Organic Sales Growth:** 6% YoY
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 17.6% (up 20 bps from Q3 FY24)
- **Adjusted EPS:** $1.05 (up 11% from $0.95 in Q3 FY24)
- **Adjusted Free Cash Flow:** $725M (Q3) / $1.79B (YTD FY25)
- **Net Debt/EBITDA:** 2.5x (within target range of 2.0-2.5x)

### Orders & Backlog
- **Orders Growth:** 2% YoY organic growth
  - Americas: +5%
  - EMEA: +2%
  - APAC: -8% (softness in China)
- **Backlog:** $14.6B (up 11% YoY, record levels)
  - Systems backlog: +11%
  - Service backlog: +8%

## Segment Performance

### Americas
- **Sales:** $4.0B (up 7% organic)
  - Service: +4%
  - Systems: +8% (Applied +HSD, F&S +LSD)
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 18.5% (up 10 bps)
- **Backlog:** $10.3B (up 10%)

### EMEA
- **Sales:** $1.3B (up 4% organic)
  - Service: +8%
  - Systems: +1%
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 14.1% (up 100 bps)
- **Backlog:** $2.6B (up 9%)

### APAC
- **Sales:** $737M (up 6% organic)
  - Service: +11%
  - Systems: +3%
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 19.4% (up 70 bps)
- **Backlog:** $1.7B (up 14%)

## Strategic Initiatives

### New Business System
CEO Joakim Weidemanis (140 days in role) is implementing a new business system focused on:
- **SIMPLIFY:** 80/20 operating model to sharpen focus on customers
- **ACCELERATE:** Lean principles to eliminate waste and drive speed
- **SCALE:** Digitization and AI to scale learnings and successes

### Early Examples of Progress
1. **Commercial (HVAC):** Path to doubling selling time for sales teams by streamlining internal processes
2. **Operations (Data Center Products):** Working to cut lead times in half, improving competitiveness and creating manufacturing capacity

### Portfolio Review
- Strategic review ongoing (2+ months into process)
- Evaluating portfolio for targeted acquisitions or thoughtful exits
- 10-15% of portfolio potentially non-core

## Guidance

### Q4 FY2025 Outlook
- **Organic Revenue:** Low single-digit growth
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** ~18.6%
- **Adjusted EPS:** $1.14 to $1.17

### Full Year FY2025 Guidance (Raised)
- **Organic Revenue:** Mid-single-digit growth (unchanged)
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** Up ~90 bps (unchanged)
- **Adjusted EPS:** $3.65 to $3.68 (raised from ~$3.60)
- **Adjusted Free Cash Flow Conversion:** >100% (raised from ~100%)

### Additional FY2025 Guidance Items

- **Amortization Expense:** ~$460M
- **Net Financing Charges:** ~$325M
- **Tax Rate:** ~12%
- **Shares:** ~660M
