# Johnson Controls International (JCI) – Q4 FY2025 Earnings Presentation

**Earnings Date:** November 5, 2025  
**Fiscal Year End:** September 30, 2025  
**Reporting Period:** Q4 FY2025 (July-September 2025)

---

## Executive Summary

### Q4 FY2025 Results

**Key Metrics:**
- **Sales:** $6.44B (up 3% YoY, +4% organic)
- **Adjusted EPS:** $1.26 (up 14% YoY from $1.11)
- **GAAP EPS:** $0.42 (includes $0.84 of special items)
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 18.8% (up 20 bps YoY)
- **Adjusted Free Cash Flow:** $710M (89% conversion in Q4, 102% for full year)

**Full Year FY2025 Results:**
- **Sales:** $23.6B (up 3% YoY, +6% organic)
- **Adjusted EPS:** $3.76 (up 17% YoY from $3.21)
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 17.1% (up 100 bps YoY)
- **Adjusted Free Cash Flow:** $2.50B (102% conversion)

### FY2026 Guidance

**Q1 FY2026:**
- Organic Revenue: Up ~3%
- Adjusted EPS: ~$0.83
- Operating Leverage: ~55%
- Adjusted Free Cash Flow Conversion: ~100%

**Full Year FY2026:**
- Organic Revenue: Up ~MSD (mid-single digits)
- Adjusted EPS: ~$4.55 (up 21% YoY from $3.76)
- Operating Leverage: ~50%
- Adjusted Free Cash Flow Conversion: ~100%

## Key Highlights

### 1. Strong Full-Year Performance
- **Organic Sales Growth:** 6% with broad-based strength
- **Margin Expansion:** 100 bps adjusted segment EBITA margin expansion to 17.1%
- **EPS Growth:** Adjusted EPS up 17% to $3.76
- **Cash Flow:** 102% adjusted free cash flow conversion

### 2. Record Backlog and Orders
- **Backlog:** Grew 13% to ~$15B (record levels)
  - Service backlog: $2.6B (+9%)
  - Systems backlog: $10.3B (+10%)
- **Orders:** Grew 7% for the year
  - Service orders: +7%
  - Systems orders: +7%

### 3. Operational Improvements
- **Business System:** Proprietary business system advancing - 80/20 and Lean principles
- **Engagement:** Over 700 colleagues involved, 200 leaders trained globally
- **Digital Enablement:** AI tools improving sales productivity and operational efficiency

### 4. Technology Leadership
- **CDU Platform:** Coolant Distribution Unit launch in September 2025 (500kW to 10MW)
- **Accelsius Investment:** Strategic investment in two-phase, direct-to-chip cooling
- **Heat Pump Project:** One of Europe's largest zero GWP ammonia refrigerant heat pump projects (~15,000 homes in Zürich)

## Segment Performance

### Americas
- **Sales:** $4.33B (up 3% organic)
- **Orders:** Service +3%, Systems +12%
- **Backlog:** $10.6B (+13%)
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 19.9% (up 50 bps YoY)
- **Highlights:** Applied HVAC +HSD growth, F&S +LSD growth

### EMEA
- **Sales:** $1.34B (up 9% organic)
- **Orders:** Service +14%, Systems -6%
- **Backlog:** $2.5B (+12%)
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 15.6% (up 30 bps YoY)
- **Highlights:** Applied HVAC +30% growth, strong service performance

### APAC
- **Sales:** $780M (down 3% organic)
- **Orders:** Service +5%, Systems -4%
- **Backlog:** $1.8B (+17%)
- **Adjusted Segment EBITA Margin:** 17.8% (down 190 bps YoY)
- **Highlights:** China weakness continuing, service growth offsetting systems decline
