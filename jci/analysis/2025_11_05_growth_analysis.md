## Executive Summary

**BLUF: Johnson Controls demonstrates **accelerating growth** with FY2025 revenue of $23.6B showing 6.0% organic growth (exceeding mid-single-digit target), driven by strong execution and market tailwinds. Historical growth shows recovery from FY2022 trough with 5-year revenue CAGR of 0.5% (reflecting cyclical recovery), but FY2025 shows acceleration with 17% adjusted EPS growth and 100 bps margin expansion. Key growth catalysts include **record $15B backlog** (+13% YoY), data center growth (~10% of sales, double-digit growth), lean transformation execution, and market tailwinds (energy efficiency, building modernization). Growth is **quality-focused** with margin expansion (+100 bps to 17.1%) and strong cash generation (102% FCF conversion). **Recommendation: BUY** - Accelerating growth with strong execution validating transformation thesis.**

## Conclusion

Johnson Controls demonstrates **accelerating growth** with FY2025 revenue of $23.6B showing 6.0% organic growth, driven by strong execution and market tailwinds. Historical growth shows recovery from FY2022 trough, with FY2025 showing acceleration with 17% adjusted EPS growth and 100 bps margin expansion.

Key growth catalysts include record $15B backlog (+13% YoY), data center growth (~10% of sales, double-digit growth), lean transformation execution, and market tailwinds (energy efficiency, building modernization). Growth is **quality-focused** with margin expansion (+100 bps to 17.1%) and strong cash generation (102% FCF conversion).

**Growth Outlook:**
- **Near-term (2025-2026)**: 6-8% organic revenue growth with margin expansion
- **Medium-term (2026-2028)**: 5-7% organic revenue growth with continued margin expansion
- **Long-term (2028-2030)**: 4-6% organic revenue growth with market maturity

**Investment Recommendation**: **BUY** - Accelerating growth with strong execution validating transformation thesis. Suitable for investors seeking exposure to building technology with strong fundamentals and margin expansion potential.
