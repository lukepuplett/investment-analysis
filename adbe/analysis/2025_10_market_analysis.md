# Adobe Inc. (ADBE) – Market Analysis (October 2025)

## Executive Summary

Adobe operates in the rapidly expanding digital creativity and marketing technology markets, with a combined addressable market exceeding $200 billion. The company has successfully positioned itself at the intersection of AI and creative productivity, capturing significant market share in both the creative software and marketing automation sectors. Adobe's AI-first strategy has enabled it to expand its total addressable market (TAM) and maintain pricing power while driving customer retention and expansion.
