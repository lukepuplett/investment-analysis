# Adobe Inc. (ADBE) – Growth Analysis

**Date:** October 2025  
**Analyst:** Investment Analysis Repository  
**Most Recent Quarter:** Q3 2025 (Ended August 30, 2025)

## Executive Summary

**BLUF: Adobe demonstrates **strong and accelerating growth** with 10% YoY revenue growth in Q3 2025, driven by successful AI transformation. AI-influenced ARR exceeded $5B (up from $3.5B at FY24 end), representing 22% of total revenue and growing 25%+. Historical 5-year revenue CAGR of 13.2% with consistent double-digit growth. Growth is **high-quality**: 96% recurring revenue, 89% gross margins, predominantly organic. Key growth catalysts include AI monetization acceleration, enterprise adoption, market expansion ($93B → $180B TAM by 2030), and international penetration. **Recommendation: Strong Buy** - Sustainable growth trajectory with accelerating AI contribution and expanding addressable market.**

## Growth Drivers by Segment

### Digital Media Segment
**Revenue:** $4.46B Q3 2025 (11% YoY growth)  
**ARR:** $18.59B (11.7% YoY growth)

**Primary Growth Drivers:**
1. **AI Integration**: Creative Cloud Pro migration driven by AI value
   - Firefly app MAU grew 30% QoQ
   - Acrobat AI Assistant units up 40% QoQ
   - Express business units up 4x YoY
2. **Premium Migration**: Users upgrading to Pro tiers for AI features
3. **New Customer Acquisition**: AI-first products attracting new users
   - 20% QoQ growth in first-time Adobe subscribers via Firefly
4. **Enterprise Adoption**: Strong enterprise Creative Cloud adoption
5. **International Expansion**: Growing international subscriber base

**Growth Quality:**
- **Recurring Revenue**: 100% subscription-based
- **High Retention**: Strong customer retention rates
- **Pricing Power**: Premium pricing on AI features
- **Cross-Sell**: Opportunities across Creative Cloud portfolio

### Digital Experience Segment
**Revenue:** $1.48B Q3 2025 (8.8% YoY growth)  
**Subscription Revenue:** $1.37B (11% YoY growth)

**Primary Growth Drivers:**
1. **Adobe Experience Platform (AEP)**: ARR growing >40% YoY
   - 70% of eligible customers using AI Assistant
   - Strong enterprise adoption
2. **Gen Studio Components**: Exceeded $1B ARR (25% YoY growth)
   - AI-powered content creation
   - Enterprise demand for AI workflows
3. **Cross-Cloud Deals**: 60% YoY growth in multi-cloud contracts
4. **Enterprise AI**: LLM Optimizer and Agent Orchestrator adoption
5. **Market Share Gains**: Growing share in marketing technology

**Growth Quality:**
- **Enterprise Focus**: High-value enterprise contracts
- **Long-term Contracts**: Multi-year enterprise agreements
- **Platform Integration**: Integrated platform driving adoption
- **AI Differentiation**: AI-first solutions creating competitive advantage

## Conclusion

Adobe demonstrates **strong and sustainable growth** with a 5-year CAGR of 13.2% and accelerating AI contribution. The company's growth is **high-quality** (96% recurring revenue, 89% gross margins, predominantly organic) and supported by strong competitive advantages. Key growth catalysts include AI monetization acceleration, enterprise adoption, market expansion ($93B → $180B TAM by 2030), and international penetration.

**Growth Outlook:**
- **Near-term (2025-2026)**: 10-12% revenue growth with AI acceleration
- **Medium-term (2026-2028)**: 12-14% revenue growth as AI matures
- **Long-term (2028-2030)**: 10-12% revenue growth with market expansion

**Investment Recommendation**: **Strong Buy** - Adobe represents one of the best growth opportunities in the software sector with sustainable, high-quality growth supported by AI transformation and market leadership.
