## Executive Summary

Adobe maintains a dominant competitive position across its core markets, with significant advantages in AI integration, ecosystem lock-in, and enterprise relationships. ## Direct Competitors Analysis

### Creative Software Competitors

#### Canva
- **Market Share**: ~15%
- **Strengths**:
  - Ease of use and accessibility
  - Strong template library
  - Affordable pricing
  - Strong SMB adoption
- **Weaknesses**:
  - Limited professional features
  - No AI integration
  - Limited enterprise capabilities
  - Weak ecosystem integration
- **Threat Level**: Medium (SMB market)

#### Figma
- **Market Share**: ~8%
- **Strengths**:
  - Real-time collaboration
  - Web-based platform
  - Strong design community
  - Good for UI/UX design
- **Weaknesses**:

  - No marketing integration
  - Limited enterprise features

- **Threat Level**: Low (now owned by Adobe)

- **Market Share**: ~5%
