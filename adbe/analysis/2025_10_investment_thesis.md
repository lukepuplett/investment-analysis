# Adobe Inc. (ADBE) – Investment Thesis (October 2025)

## Executive Summary

Adobe represents a compelling investment opportunity as the leading provider of creative and marketing software with a successful AI-first transformation strategy. The company's strong competitive moats, exceptional financial performance, and expanding addressable market position it for sustained growth in the AI era. With AI-influenced ARR surpassing $5 billion and strong forward guidance, Adobe is well-positioned to capture significant value from the AI revolution while maintaining its market leadership.
