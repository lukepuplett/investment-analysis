## Waymo (GOOGL) Comparison

**Scenario outcomes (probability-weighted):**
- A-weighted: 0.50 × $30–90B = **$15–45B**
- B-weighted: 0.35 × $150–400B = **$52.5–140B**
- C-weighted: 0.15 × $2–10T = **$300B–1.5T**
- **Expected value: $368B–1.69T (mid-range ~$700B)**
- **Per share: +$30–140** (vs. current +$2–6 in base case)

**Key divergence:**
- Regime A: Waymo dominates premium metros; Tesla holds suburbs
- Regime B: Waymo's regulatory moat widens; Tesla under pressure
- Regime C: Winner-take-most; could be either (depends on FSD generalization vs. Waymo's regulatory lead)

## Tesla (TSLA) Comparison

**Scenario outcomes (probability-weighted):**
- A-weighted: 0.50 × $15–50B = **$7.5–25B**
- B-weighted: 0.35 × $80–200B = **$28–70B**
- C-weighted: 0.15 × ($500B–5T OR $0) = **$75B–750B or $0**
- **Expected value: $111B–845B (if wins in C) or $35B–95B (if loses)**

**Key divergence:**
- Regime A: Tesla survives with cost advantage in suburbs; margins tight
- Regime B: Tesla's autonomy race matters; Waymo's moat tests him
- Regime C: All-or-nothing. Tesla wins if FSD stacks perfectly. Loses if Waymo's regulatory lead insurmountable.

**Regime A holds if (all true):**
- Ownership substitution stalled <5%
- Approval pace unchanged 3–6%/year

- Margins don't expand (take-rate 18–32%)

**Regime B holds if (all true):**
