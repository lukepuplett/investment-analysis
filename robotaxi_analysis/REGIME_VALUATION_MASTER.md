## Divergence Points

| Observed by | Signal | Action |
|-------------|--------|--------|
| Q4 2027 | Ownership <5% in all metros | Lock in Regime A; eliminate B/C/D |
| Q4 2027 | Ownership 10–25% trending up | Shift probability mass to B |
| Q4 2028 | Ownership >30% in 2+ metros | Regime C likely; re-model |
| Q4 2027 | Approval pace <3%/year | Lock in Regime A |
| Q4 2027 | Approval pace >8%/year sustained | Confirm B inflection |

| Shift | Waymo Expected Value Change | Tesla Expected Value Change |

| C+5% → D+5% | +$695B | +$28B |

Waymo is most sensitive to B→C shifts. Tesla is most sensitive to B probability (congestion limits D upside for US-only model).
