### 3. Competitive Analysis (Updated)
**BLUF:** Rocket Lab maintains near-uncontested dominance in small-lift dedicated launch (<500kg to orbit) with 100% success rate (21 missions 2025), established customer base spanning government/commercial, and superior launch cadence vs. all competitors. However, competitive threats intensify at medium-lift: SpaceX Falcon 9 rideshare economics constrain Electron TAM; Relativity Space (3D-printed medium-lift) and Blue Origin New Glenn (super-heavy) threaten Neutron's market window if RKLB fails to launch operationally by Q4 2026. RKLB's 12-18 month first-mover advantage on medium-lift is CRITICAL; delay beyond Q2 2027 hands market to competitors. Recommendation: Small-lift moat is durable; medium-lift success is existential within 3-year window.
