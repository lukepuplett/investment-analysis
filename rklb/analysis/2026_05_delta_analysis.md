## WHAT CHANGED SINCE Q4 2025

### 1. Revenue & Growth Acceleration ✅ VALIDATED

**Q4 Analysis Expected:** Electron steady, Space Systems ramp, government contracts strong  
**Q1 Reality:**
- **Electron:** 31 launches booked in Q1 (record quarter)—up from steady baseline
- **Space Systems:** $136.7M revenue (68% of total), +57.2% YoY, +31.7% sequential
- **HASTE:** $190M contract (20-launch order) is largest single order in program history
- **Verdict:** EXCEEDED EXPECTATIONS. Both Electron and Space Systems accelerating beyond Q4 projections.

**Impact on Thesis:** Space Systems growth engine pillar (Thesis Pillar 1, 8/10 confidence) now validated at higher acceleration rates. Q1 sequential growth (+11.3%) suggests momentum continuing.

---

### 2. Backlog Explosion & Contract Wins ✅ MAJOR VALIDATION

**Q4 Analysis Expected:** Backlog $1.85B, steady government/commercial mix, SDA Tranche 3 executed  
**Q1 Reality:**
- **Total Backlog:** $2.2B (+$350M in one quarter, +19% Q1 organic)
- **Launch Backlog:** 41.5% of total ($912M estimated); 70+ missions in backlog (record)
- **Largest Contract Ever:** 5 Neutron + 3 Electron flights, 2029 delivery (confidential customer, commercial rates)
- **HASTE:** $190M/20-launch order (DoW/MACH-TB program)
- **Neutron:** 5+ flight commitments from single customer (validates demand narrative)
- **Verdict:** DRAMATICALLY EXCEEDED. Backlog +$350M in 3 months; 36% conversion rate suggests $720M-800M revenue in next 12 months.

**Impact on Thesis:** Government Contracting Advantage (Thesis Pillar 4, 7/10 confidence) and Neutron 5–10x Upside (Pillar 3, 4/10) both significantly de-risked. Largest contract proves Neutron demand is real, not speculative.

---

### 3. Neutron Development Progress ✅ ON TRACK

**Q4 Analysis Expected:** Tank rupture (Jan 2026) resolved; static fire tests Q1-Q2; first flight Q4 2026 target intact  
**Q1 Reality:**
- **Stage 1 Tank:** Design refinements complete; AFP (Automated Fiber Placement) components already in production
- **Stage Separation:** Tests cleared at full flight loads; Hungry Hippo fairing deployment validated
- **Archimedes Engines:** Continuous hot-fire testing at Stenlis (Stage 1 + vacuum variants)
- **Return On Investment (ROI) Barge:** 11 million pounds, 10 MW power generation, sea trials later in 2026
- **Booster Recovery Timeline:** Soft splashdown Flight 1 (nominal), barge landing Flight 2 (reusable architecture)
- **Verdict:** TRACKING. Despite January composite tank rupture, team has closed design, initiated production, and cleared separation events.

**Impact on Thesis:** Neutron technical risk (Thesis Pillar 3, 4/10 confidence) moves to 5-6/10 confidence. AFP production signals transition from development to manufacturing—structural (not just testing) progress. However, booster recovery (Flight 2) and composite structure at scale remain unproven.

---

### 4. Acquisitions & Vertical Integration ✅ ACCELERATING

**Q4 Analysis Expected:** GEOST synergies driving SDA win; additional acquisitions in pipeline  
**Q1 Reality:**
- **Motiv Space Systems:** Definitive agreement announced (mechanisms, robotics, spacecraft subsystems)
  - Heritage: Mars Perseverance rover arm, zoom/focus mechanisms
  - Unlocks lunar/planetary missions (Commercial Mars Sample Return, future Artemis)
  - Adds in-house manufacturing of solar array drive assemblies, antenna gimbals, filter wheels
- **SolAero (Closed):** Mynaric + optical comms + laser terminals
  - Establishes Rocket Lab Europe (German footprint, €109B EU/NATO space investment growth)
- **OURS Electric Propulsion:** 200-unit production line already operational
  - Addresses bottleneck in electric propulsion supply chain
- **Verdict:** AGGRESSIVE EXECUTION. Three major acquisitions (GEOST, Mynaric, Motiv) + one organic product launch (OURS) in single quarter.

**Impact on Thesis:** Vertical integration strategy (central to competitive moat) accelerating faster than Q4 analysis suggested. Motiv acquisition completes "satellite value chain" across optics, solar, propulsion, mechanisms. De-risks near-term SDA execution and unlocks new verticals (lunar, planetary).

---

### 5. Government Programs & Partnerships ✅ EXPANDING

**Q4 Analysis Expected:** SDA Tranche 3, Golden Dome, NASA steady; space defense bipartisan support  
**Q1 Reality:**
- **Space-Based Interceptor (Golden Dome):** RKLB selected with Raytheon for advanced capabilities demonstration
- **Anduril Partnership:** 3 HASTE launches booked (defense rapid prototyping + Rocket Lab flight cadence)
- **Electron & HASTE:** 31 Electron + 20 HASTE launches booked in Q1 alone
- **100th Launch Milestone:** On track to achieve this year (industry record)
- **Verdict:** EXCEEDED. New partnerships (Anduril) and program wins (Space-Based Interceptor) validate RKLB's positioning as "new era of space primes."

**Impact on Thesis:** Government contracting advantage deepens. HASTE as one-third of launch backlog (up from modest baseline) suggests hypersonic/defense testing is becoming material revenue stream, not niche.
