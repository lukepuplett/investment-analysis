# Rocket Lab (RKLB) Financial Analysis – Q4 2025 Results
**Analysis Date:** February 26, 2026

---

## Executive Summary (BLUF)

**Rocket Lab delivered exceptional Q4 2025 and full-year results with record revenue of $602M (+38% YoY), record backlog of $1.85B (+73% YoY), and record gross margins of 38% GAAP (44% non-GAAP). Space Systems revenue growth and margin expansion (particularly from GEOST integration) are the primary financial engines, not Electron. The company maintains $1.01B cash with -$275.73M trailing FCF, indicating continued cash burn despite strong revenue growth. Neutron's Q4 2026 first flight timeline requires sustained capital deployment. Recommendation: Strong financial execution supports 2026 guidance; monitor Space Systems margin sustainability (target 40%+) and Neutron development costs.**

## Conclusion

Rocket Lab's FY 2025 financial performance demonstrates strong execution across all business lines, with revenue growth of 38% YoY and record backlog of $1.85B. Space Systems has emerged as the primary profit driver, with gross margins expanding to 38% GAAP through GEOST integration and vertical integration. The company maintains adequate liquidity ($1B+) and is approaching operating profitability (operating margin -16% in 2025, expected -8% to breakeven by 2026-2027).

**Investment Implication:** Financial health is strong enough to support Neutron development while scaling Space Systems. Key monitoring points are Space Systems margin sustainability and Neutron development cost discipline.
