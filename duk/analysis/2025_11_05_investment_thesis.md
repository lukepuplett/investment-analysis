**Brookfield Investment in Duke Energy Florida:**
- **Value**: $6B for 19.7% stake

**Piedmont Tennessee Sale:**
- **Value**: $2.48B to Spire

**Combined Impact:**
- **Net Proceeds**: ~$7.5B
- **Credit Strengthening**: FFO/Debt target raised to 15%
- **Capital Efficiency**: Premium valuations provide efficient equity funding
