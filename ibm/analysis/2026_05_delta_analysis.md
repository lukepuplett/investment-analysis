# IBM Delta Analysis (2026-05)

**Purpose:** Capture material changes since prior period to enable focused quarterly monitoring  
**Comparison Period:** Q1 2026 vs Q1 2025 + Full Year 2025 vs 2024

---

## Executive Summary (BLUF)

IBM's story in 2025-2026 is **transformation acceleration**. The company went from "growth is flat, who cares" (2024) to "operational leverage is kicking in, pay attention" (2025-2026). Key shifts: (1) **Margin expansion became visible** (gross margin +300 bps structural shift), (2) **EPS growth detached from revenue growth** (73% EPS growth on 7.5% revenue growth), (3) **M&A deployed at scale** (Confluent acquisition changed growth narrative), (4) **Mainframe re-ignited** (AI workloads driving demand), (5) **AI platform launching** (watsonx adoption beginning). **Thesis validation:** All major thesis pillars are **tracking as expected**. No red flags yet.

### Margin Expansion (The Hidden Story)

| Metric | Q1 2025 | Q1 2026 | Change (bps) | Implication |

| **Gross Margin %** | 55.22% | 58.36% | **+314 bps** | Structural shift (software mix) |
| **Operating Margin %** | 14.46% | 18.51% | **+405 bps** | Operating leverage kicking in |
| **Net Margin %** | 7.26% | 7.64% | **+38 bps** | Bottom line expanding |

**Delta Assessment:** ✅ **Margin expansion is the REAL story, not revenue growth.** This is what's driving 73% EPS growth on 9.5% revenue growth. Key validation: SG&A declined $77M YoY while revenue grew $1.4B — that's operational leverage in action.

**Sustainability Risk:** ⚠️ **If software mix stalls or competition intensifies, margins could compress.** This is the #1 risk to thesis.

## Thesis Changes Needed

### No Major Changes Recommended

The analysis confirms the thesis remains intact:
- ✅ Financial power is real (margin expansion, operational leverage)
- ✅ Market tailwinds are structural (data sovereignty, AI infrastructure)
- ✅ Valuation is fair (20.5x P/E appropriate for 8% growth)
- ⚠️ Execution risks remain (M&A integration, growth sustainability)

### Refinements / Nuances

1. **Mainframe +48% is probably not sustainable** — might have been easier comp or mix shift. Expect to normalize to 15-20% in subsequent quarters. **Not a red flag, just normalization.**

2. **Confluent adoption is early** — need 2-3 quarters of results to confirm integration success. Red Hat precedent suggests it will work, but not yet proven.

3. **watsonx adoption is critical path** — If watsonx doesn't reach $500M revenue by 2027, growth thesis damaged. This is the #1 thing to watch.

4. **Hybrid cloud competitive window is narrowing** — AWS/Azure improving offerings. IBM's 2-3 year window assumption might be 1-2 years. Acceleration required.
