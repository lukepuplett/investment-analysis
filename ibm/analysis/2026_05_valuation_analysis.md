## Executive Summary (BLUF)

IBM at 20.5x P/E is **fairly valued for a 7-8% grower**, not cheap. The stock appreciated from $220 (Feb lows) to $233 (now) due to earnings delivery, not multiple expansion. **Further upside requires:** (1) acceleration of growth to 10%+, OR (2) margin expansion surprise beyond current 58% gross margin, OR (3) successful M&A accretion (Confluent). At current valuation, **expected return is 6-9% annually** (dividend + modest growth), consistent with a mature tech company. **Not a screaming buy, but not overvalued either.**
