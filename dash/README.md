## Business Overview

DoorDash is a technology company that connects consumers with local merchants through its on-demand delivery platform. The company operates in three primary segments:

### Core Business Segments
1. **Marketplace** - Platform connecting consumers, merchants, and Dashers (delivery drivers)
2. **DoorDash Drive** - White-label delivery service for merchants
3. **DoorDash Storefront** - Online ordering platform for merchants

### Key Products & Services
- Food delivery from restaurants
- Grocery delivery
- Convenience store delivery
- Alcohol delivery
- Pet supplies and other retail categories
- Merchant services and technology solutions

## Repository Organization

### Directory Structure
- `analysis/`: Contains dated analysis documents with detailed research, market analysis, and investment thesis
- `financials/`: Organized by month, contains:
  * Balance sheet analysis
  * Income statement analysis
  * Cash flow analysis
  * Yahoo Finance statistics and metrics
- `quarterly/`: Contains quarterly financial statements and reports:
  * SEC Form 10-Q filings
  * Earnings presentations
  * Quarterly performance summaries

## File Naming Convention

- Analysis documents: `YYYY_MM_DD_[type].md`
  * financial_analysis
  * market_analysis
  * competitive_analysis
  * technical_analysis
  * risk_assessment
  * investment_thesis
  * valuation_analysis
- Financial documents: Organized by month in `YYYY_MM/` folders
- Quarterly statements: `YYYY_Q#_[type].md` format where type is:
  * `form_10Q` for SEC filings
  * `presentation` for earnings presentations
  * `press_release` for earnings press releases
