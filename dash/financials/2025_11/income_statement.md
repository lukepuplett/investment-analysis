## Key Observations

### Revenue Growth Trajectory
- **Strong 5-year CAGR:** 19.4% revenue growth
- **2024 Revenue:** $10.7B (up 24.2% YoY)
- **TTM Revenue:** $11.9B (continuing growth trajectory)
- Consistent double-digit growth throughout period

### Profitability Journey
- **2021-2022:** Operating losses (-9.2% to -15.7% operating margin)
- **2023:** Approaching breakeven (-6.7% operating margin)
- **2024:** Near breakeven (-0.4% operating margin)
- **TTM:** **Profitable (4.6% operating margin)** - Significant milestone

### Margin Expansion Story
- **Gross Margin:** Improved from 45.5% (2022) to 50.0% (TTM)
- **Operating Margin:** Improved from -15.7% (2022) to +4.6% (TTM)
- **Net Margin:** Improved from -20.7% (2022) to +6.6% (TTM)

### Operating Leverage
- **Selling & Marketing:** Declining as % of revenue (33.1% → 18.6%)
- **R&D:** Stable as % of revenue (~10-11%)
- **G&A:** Declining as % of revenue (16.3% → 11.4%)
- **Cost of Revenue:** Improving efficiency (54.5% → 50.0%)

### Path to Profitability
1. **2021-2022:** Investment phase with significant losses
2. **2023:** Approaching breakeven, improving unit economics
3. **2024:** Near breakeven, strong revenue growth
4. **TTM:** **Profitable** - Operating leverage materializing
