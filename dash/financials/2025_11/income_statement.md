## Key Observations

### Revenue Growth
- Revenue increased 27% Y/Y to $3.4 billion
- Accelerating growth rate (21% in Q1 → 25% in Q2 → 27% in Q3)
- Net Revenue Margin expanded to 13.8% (+30 bps Y/Y, +30 bps Q/Q)

### Profitability Expansion
- Gross margin: 49.0% (up from 47.4% in Q3 2024)
- Contribution margin: 36.8% (up from 34.4% in Q3 2024)
- Adjusted EBITDA margin: 3.0% of Marketplace GOV (up from 2.7% in Q3 2024)
- Operating margin: 7.5% (up from 4.0% in Q3 2024)

### Operating Leverage
- Revenue growth (+27%) outpaced expense growth (+22.7%)
- Sales and marketing as % of revenue: 16.7% (down from 17.9% in Q3 2024)
- R&D as % of revenue: 10.3% (down from 10.7% in Q3 2024)

### Cash Generation
- Operating cash flow: $871 million in Q3 2025 (up from $531 million in Q3 2024)
- Free Cash Flow: $723 million in Q3 2025 (up from $444 million in Q3 2024)
