## Key Observations

### Cash Position
- **Total cash and marketable securities:** $8.3 billion (up from $5.5 billion)
- **Restricted cash increased significantly:** $4.1 billion (up from $190 million)
  - Likely related to Deliveroo acquisition escrow/restrictions
- **Cash and cash equivalents decreased:** $3.3 billion (down from $4.0 billion)
  - Used for Deliveroo acquisition and operations

### Acquisition Impact (Deliveroo)
- **Goodwill increased:** $3.6 billion (up from $2.3 billion)
  - Reflects Deliveroo acquisition premium
- **Intangible assets increased:** $878 million (up from $510 million)
  - Acquired intangibles from Deliveroo
- **Convertible notes issued:** $2.7 billion
  - Financing component of Deliveroo acquisition

### Balance Sheet Strength
- **Strong liquidity:** $8.3 billion in cash and marketable securities
- **Net cash position:** $5.6 billion (after accounting for convertible notes)
- **Current ratio:** 2.04 (strong liquidity position)
- **Working capital:** $5.4 billion (strong operating capital)

### Asset Growth
- **Total assets:** $18.0 billion (up from $12.8 billion, +39.9%)
- Primarily driven by:
  - Deliveroo acquisition ($3.6 billion goodwill + $368 million intangibles)
  - Increased restricted cash ($3.9 billion)
  - Property and equipment investments ($198 million)
