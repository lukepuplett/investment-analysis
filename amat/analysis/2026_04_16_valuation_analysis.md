## Executive Summary (BLUF)

**BLUF: Applied Materials is trading at 40.4x trailing P/E and 36.2x forward P/E, well above semiconductor equipment sector average (~25x) but justified by 20%+ expected 2026 growth and 49%+ gross margins. DCF valuation yields $520-580 range (base case $540), suggesting 37-47% upside from current $394 price. Valuation is sustainable if company executes on 20%+ growth in 2026-2027 and maintains 49%+ margins through capacity constraints. Fair value range: $520-580. Upside scenario (bull case) $600+. Downside protection limited at ~$380 if growth disappoints. Valuation verdict: Fairly valued to slightly undervalued relative to long-term value creation opportunity.**
