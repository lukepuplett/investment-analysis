# Parker-Hannifin (PH) - Financial Analysis (Q2 FY2026)

## BLUF

**PH demonstrates exceptional financial momentum with record Q2 revenues of $5.2B (+9% YoY) and organic growth of 6.6%, driven by aerospace surge (+13.5% organic) and industrial recovery. Adjusted segment operating margins expanded 150 bps to 27.1%, reflecting operational excellence. However, the -11% earnings decline YoY (vs +9% revenue growth) signals margin pressure from integration costs and acquisition expenses, offset by record adjusted EPS growth of +17%. Guidance raised to $30.40-$31.00 adjusted EPS (FY2026), supported by $11.7B record backlog. Valuation concerns at 36x P/E and 24.5x EV/EBITDA merit scrutiny, though backlog visibility justifies premium.**

## Financial Health Assessment

### Strengths
- **Record Margins**: 27.1% adjusted segment margin (up 150 bps) across both segments
- **Strong Organic Growth**: 6.6% organic growth demonstrates underlying demand strength
- **Exceptional Aerospace Performance**: 13.5% organic growth with 30.2% margins
- **Backlog Visibility**: $11.7B backlog provides multi-year revenue visibility
- **Robust FCF Generation**: $2.47B levered FCF TTM (66% conversion)
- **Moderate Leverage**: 69% Debt/Equity with 5.6x interest coverage

### Concerns
- **Valuation**: Trailing P/E of 36x and EV/EBITDA of 24.5x are premium
- **Earnings-Revenue Divergence**: -11% reported earnings YoY vs +9% revenue growth
- **Inventory Build**: Up 11% to $3.2B (integration or demand?)
- **Integration Risk**: Curtis ($1B) integrating, Filtration Group ($9.25B) pending
- **Leverage for M&A**: Debt/Equity will increase with Filtration Group acquisition
- **Cyclical Exposure**: Industrial segment dependent on economic recovery continuation
