# Parker-Hannifin Corp (PH) – Technical Analysis

**Date:** August 15, 2025  
**Period Analyzed:** Fiscal Year 2025 (ended June 30, 2025)

---

## Executive Summary

**BLUF: Parker-Hannifin demonstrates strong technical capabilities across motion and control technologies with 85% IP coverage, proprietary designs on premier aerospace programs, and interconnected technology portfolio. The Win Strategy™ provides operational excellence framework enabling consistent margin expansion. Technical risks include technology disruption (electrification, digitalization), integration challenges (Meggitt, Curtis), and R&D investment requirements. However, strong innovation culture, application engineering expertise, and strategic acquisitions position company well for technology evolution. Technical capabilities are a competitive strength supporting market leadership. BUY - Strong technical foundation with innovation culture supporting competitive advantage.**
