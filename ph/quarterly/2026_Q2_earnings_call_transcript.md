Earnings call

Parker-Hannifin reported FY26 Q2 record sales of $5.2B (+9% YoY), 27.1% adjusted segment operating margin (+150 bps), and $7.65 EPS (+17%). Management raised FY26 guidance across sales, margins, and EPS, citing strong aerospace growth (+14.5% YoY), off-highway recovery, and robust backlog. The Filtration Group acquisition is expected to add $220M in synergies and expand Parker’s aftermarket and life sciences exposure.
