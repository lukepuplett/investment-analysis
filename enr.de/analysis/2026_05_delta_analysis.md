## Executive Summary

**BLUF: Since entering Siemens Energy coverage in Q1 FY2026, multiple positive catalysts have validated the investment thesis, with several metrics materially stronger than baseline expectations. Key positives: record orders (€17.6B, +34% YoY), record backlog (€146B, +22.7% YoY), accelerating FCF (€2.9B Q1, suggesting €5.5B+ annualized), Americas emerging as growth driver (orders +60%), and SG 7.0 first commercial order announced. Key areas of caution: Q1 margin benefits from timing effects (may not repeat), Gamesa path to breakeven remains uncertain, US capex execution not yet proven. Overall thesis remains intact and strengthening; execution risks are real but manageable. Conviction on energy transition tailwind (9/10) and backlog quality (8/10) unchanged. Valuation remains fair (€215-250 range).**
