**WACC Calculation:**
- Cost of Equity: 8% risk-free + 2% equity risk premium (vs. S&P 500 4-5%) = 10%

- WACC = (70% × 10%) + (30% × 3.5% × 0.8) = 7% + 0.84% = 7.84% ≈ **8.0%** (using 8.0% instead of 10.0% for upside scenario; 10.0% for base)
