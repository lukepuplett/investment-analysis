# Laser Photonics Corporation (LASE) – Financial Analysis (June 2026)

## Executive Summary (BLUF)

**LASE demonstrates strong top-line growth (+144% YoY to $8.3M in 2025) and successful CMS acquisition integration, but faces critical financial headwinds: deeply negative net income (-$17.5M TTM), severe cash burn (-$6.4M operating CF), negative equity (-$5.0M), and cash position depleted 94% from $12.2M (end 2022) to $0.65M (end 2025). Path to profitability remains unclear despite gross margin stability at 14.4%. Company entirely dependent on capital raises ($3.5M equity + $7.5M debt in 2025) to fund operations. High execution risk on backlog conversion and cost leverage; unlikely to achieve cash flow positivity in 2026 absent significant operational improvements or M&A catalysts.**

**Path to Improvement:**
