## Red Flags to Monitor

### High-Priority Watch Items

1. **Revenue Miss vs. Backlog**: If Q2/Q3 2026 revenue falls below 50% of backlog expectations, backlog conversion failure risk rises
2. **Gross Margin Decline**: If GM deteriorates below 14% in 2026, path to profitability in jeopardy
3. **Cash Depletion**: If cash falls below $0.3M without capital raise announced, liquidity crisis imminent
4. **LSAD Delays**: If trials slip beyond Q4 2026, program momentum may stall
5. **Customer Loss**: If any top-5 customer (especially J&J) reduces orders >20%, concentration risk realized

### Green Flags to Confirm Thesis

1. **Backlog Conversion >$1.5M in H1 2026**: Validates customer demand
2. **Gross Margin Recovery to 16%+**: Facility savings + mix improvement working
3. **Capital Raise Completed >$4M**: Extends runway to end 2027
4. **LSAD Trials Initiated On-Time**: Program advancing as expected
5. **New Navy/Army Orders >$1M**: Defense segment scaling
