## Notes for analysis

- **2025 current debt / other current borrowings** (~$1.29B) dominates the leap in **current liabilities** and **total debt**; tie to filings (e.g. convertible classification, covenant summaries) rather than interpreting as revolver draws without the 10-K note.
- **Deferred revenue** (current + non-current) remains a key subscription health indicator on the liability side.
- **Cash + short-term investments** (~$4.1B FY2025 in this feed) should be read beside **investing flows** and **weighted avg maturity** from the annual report—vendor “cash” vs “restricted” lines can diverge slightly from cash flow statement **end cash** labels.
- Reconcile **`Properties` = 0** in the scrape to the audited breakdown if material; often a mapping quirk in the vendor tree.
