## Key Observations

### Revenue Growth & Scale
- **Strong top-line growth:** 29.85% YoY revenue increase in TTM, with acceleration from 2022 base
- **Revenue:** $2.17B (TTM), up from $975M in 2022 — 122% CAGR over 3 years
- Demonstrates strong market demand and product-market fit

### Gross Margin Dynamics
- **Healthy gross margins:** 74.51% (TTM) showing strong pricing power and operational leverage
- **Slight compression:** from 77.31% (2024) to 74.51% (TTM) — potential cost inflation or mix shift
- Gross profit growing faster than revenue suggests improving unit economics despite compression

### Operating Leverage Challenge
- **Still unprofitable at operating level:** -$207M operating loss despite $1.6B gross profit
- **Operating expense ratio declining:** 84.07% (TTM) vs 96.78% (2022) — significant improvement trend
- **Path to profitability visible:** R&D + SG&A spending at $1.8B on $2.2B revenue; modest cuts could drive breakeven

### Non-Operating Benefits
- **Strong interest income:** $131.2M offset by $8.8M interest expense = $122.5M net benefit
- **Cash balance benefit:** Reflects $4.1B cash position earning ~3% yield
- This masks underlying GAAP losses and provides earnings support

### EBITDA Inflection
- **EBITDA turned positive:** $105.8M (TTM, 4.88% margin) vs -$36M (2023)
- **Significant improvement:** From negative EBITDA in 2023 to ~$106M suggests operating model maturing
- **Normalized EBITDA includes one-time charges removal:** Real operational performance stronger than headline

### Bottom Line Impact
- **GAAP net loss:** -$102M (TTM) but improving from -$184M (2023)
- **Loss improvement trend:** From -$193M (2022) → -$184M (2023) → -$79M (2024) → -$102M (TTM)
- **Inflection point visible:** 50% improvement from 2022 to 2024, though TTM shows slight pause

### Investment Implications
- Company shows **clear path to profitability** with top-line growth and operating leverage
- **EBITDA profitability already achieved** — gap to GAAP profitability primarily opex-driven
- **Cash generation potential** — with modest margin improvement, could reach positive GAAP earnings
- **Risk:** Operating margin expansion depends on revenue growth sustaining while controlling cost growth

*Abbreviations: TTM = Trailing Twelve Months, YoY = Year over Year, EBIT = Earnings Before Interest & Taxes, EBITDA = EBIT + Depreciation & Amortization*
