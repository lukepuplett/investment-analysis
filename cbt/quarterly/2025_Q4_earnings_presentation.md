## Outlook for Fiscal 2026

### Segment EBIT Outlook
- **Reinforcement Materials:** Outlook impacted by dynamics of Asian tire trade flows
- **Performance Chemicals:** Improving demand in key growth areas and continued optimization expected to drive EBIT growth

### Challenging Macroeconomic Backdrop
- Expecting third straight year of LV Auto Production contraction in NA & EU
- Source: IHS September 2025
- Stagnant global manufacturing PMI
- European manufacturing PMI persistently below 50 for three years
- Source: S&P Global Trading Economics as of 9/30/25

### Cash Flow and Balance Sheet Outlook
- Net debt to EBITDA ratio and liquidity expected to remain similar to 2025 levels
- Cash flows expected to fund growth investments and cash return to shareholders

### Fiscal 2026 Guidance
- **Adjusted EPS:** Expected to be $6.00 to $7.00 in fiscal 2026
- Challenging economic conditions and high competitive intensity expected to continue
- **Note:** Given the current outlook, we do not expect to meet the 2024 Investor Day financial targets.
