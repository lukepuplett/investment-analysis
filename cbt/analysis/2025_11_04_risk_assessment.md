## Executive Summary

**BLUF: Cabot faces moderate to elevated risks from cyclical market headwinds (Asian tire imports, weak auto production), competitive intensity, and macro uncertainty, but mitigates these through strong balance sheet ($1.5B liquidity, 1.2x net debt/EBITDA), operational excellence, and strategic positioning in high-growth markets. Key risks include volume declines in Reinforcement Materials, trade policy uncertainty, and technology disruption, while financial risks are low given strong cash generation and conservative leverage. Overall risk profile is moderate with manageable risks offset by strong financial position and operational capabilities. MODERATE RISK - Strong risk mitigation offsets cyclical and competitive challenges.**

## Risk Categories

### Market Risks

#### Volume Declines

**Risk Level:** Elevated

**Description:**
- Reinforcement Materials volumes down 5% YoY in FY2025
- Q4 volumes down 5% globally, with Americas down 7% and Asia Pacific down 6%
- Performance Chemicals volumes down 5% in Q4 (Europe weakness)

**Impact:**
- Revenue decline of 7% in FY2025
- Pressure on segment profitability
- Reduced asset utilization

**Mitigation:**
- Strong cost discipline offsetting volume impacts
- Margin expansion despite volume declines
- Network optimization maintaining profitability
- Strategic focus on high-growth applications

**Outlook:**
- Continued volume headwinds expected in FY2026
- Reinforcement Materials outlook impacted by Asian tire trade flows
- Performance Chemicals expected to improve in key growth areas

#### Asian Tire Imports

**Risk Level:** Elevated

**Description:**
- Elevated levels of Asian tire imports into Western markets
- Displacing local tire production and carbon black demand
- Tariff uncertainty creating volatility

**Impact:**
- Lower production levels at tire customers
- Reduced demand for carbon black in Americas
- Volume pressure on Reinforcement Materials segment

**Mitigation:**
- Working with customers on production shifts
- Supporting volume from global plant network
- Tariff policies potentially providing support
- Cost discipline enabling competitive positioning

**Outlook:**
- Trade policy uncertainty continues
- Tariff impacts uncertain in magnitude and timing
- Potential support if tariffs are sustained

#### Auto Production Weakness

**Risk Level:** Moderate to Elevated

**Description:**
- Third straight year of LV auto production contraction in NA & EU
- Weak auto production affecting tire demand
- OEM tire demand decline

**Impact:**
- Lower demand for carbon black in tire applications
- Volume pressure on Reinforcement Materials
- Reduced market growth

**Mitigation:**
- Diversification across applications
- Aftermarket tire demand more stable
- Cost discipline maintaining profitability
- Strategic focus on high-growth applications

**Outlook:**
- Auto production weakness expected to continue
- Structural shift to EVs affecting demand patterns
- Long-term adaptation required

#### European Construction Weakness

**Risk Level:** Moderate

**Description:**
- Weak European construction demand
- Performance Chemicals volumes down in Europe (construction-related applications)
- Manufacturing PMI persistently below 50 for three years

**Impact:**
- Lower demand for Performance Chemicals in Europe
- Volume pressure on segment

**Mitigation:**
- Geographic diversification
- Growth in other applications (battery materials, infrastructure)
- Cost management offsetting impacts

**Outlook:**
- European weakness expected to continue

- Long-term recovery uncertain

## Conclusion

Cabot Corporation faces moderate to elevated risks from cyclical market headwinds, competitive intensity, and macro uncertainty. However, the company's strong financial position, operational excellence, and strategic positioning provide effective risk mitigation.

Key risks include volume declines in Reinforcement Materials, trade policy uncertainty, and competitive intensity. However, financial risks are low given strong cash generation, conservative leverage, and strong liquidity position.

The company's risk mitigation capabilities are strong, demonstrated by margin expansion despite volume headwinds, cost discipline maintaining profitability, and strategic positioning in high-growth markets. Operational excellence and financial strength provide resilience against market challenges.

**Recommendation:** MODERATE RISK - Strong risk mitigation offsets cyclical and competitive challenges. Suitable for investors with tolerance for cyclical headwinds but seeking strong financial position and operational excellence.
