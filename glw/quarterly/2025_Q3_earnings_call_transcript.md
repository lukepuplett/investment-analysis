## CEO Remarks - Wendell Weeks

### Third Quarter 2025 Results

Today, we reported another excellent quarter. Year-over-year:
- Sales grew 14% to $4.27 billion
- EPS grew 24% to $0.67, again outpacing sales growth
- Operating margin expanded 130 basis points to 19.6%
- ROIC increased 160 basis points to 13.4%
- Free cash flow of $535 million puts us on track for another year of strong free cash flow growth

### Springboard Plan Progress

**Q3 2025 results versus Q4 2023 launch point:**
- Grew sales 31%
- Expanded operating margin by 330 basis points
- Grew EPS 72%, more than twice the rate of sales growth
- Expanded ROIC by 460 basis points
- Generated strong free cash flow

**Incremental Sales Growth:**
- Through Q3 2025, added $4 billion of incremental annualized sales since launch
- Q4 2025 expected sales of $4.35 billion will add another $300 million to annualized sales run rate
- Operating margin target of 20% expected to be achieved in Q4, a full year ahead of plan

### Growth Opportunities

#### Mobile Consumer Electronics - Apple Partnership
- Apple committed $2.5 billion to produce 100% of iPhone and Apple Watch cover glass in the U.S. for the first time
- Corning's Harrodsburg, Kentucky facility will become home to the world's largest and most advanced smartphone glass production line
- New Apple-Corning Innovation Center to deepen co-innovation and play key role in future generations of Apple products

#### GenAI Network "Scale-out" Opportunity
- Enterprise Network business grew sales 58% year-over-year in Q3
- Primary driver: hyperscale customers scaling out GPU clusters with more connected AI nodes
- Inventor of world's first low-loss optical fiber and technology leader in this space
- Largest producer by revenue of fiber, cable, and multi-fiber connectors in the world
- Low-cost, U.S.-based advanced manufacturing platforms for critical components
- Unique opportunity to support hyperscale AI customers building major U.S. data centers using U.S. origin products

#### GenAI Network "Scale-up" Opportunity
- Hyperscalers creating more capable nodes (moving from <100 GPUs per node to hundreds of GPUs per node)
- AI nodes shifting to stretch across multiple server racks, causing longer distances between GPUs
- Links will eventually reach ~100 gigabit per second meter (electrical to optical frontier line)
- Co-packaged optics (CPO) technology helps activate this scale-up opportunity
- Scale-up opportunity could be 2-3x the size of existing Enterprise Network business

#### GenAI Data Center Interconnect (DCI)
- Introduced high-density GenAI fiber and cable system enabling customers to fit 2-4x more fiber into existing conduit
- Began shipping in Q1, doubled sales in Q2, significant sequential step up in Q3
- Expect this business to scale rapidly, reaching $1 billion opportunity by end of decade
- Collaboration with Microsoft to accelerate production of hollow core fiber
- Hollow core technology addresses cases where speed of light through glass vs. air matters

#### Solar Business
- Built strong foundation for rapidly accelerating growth
- Made process advancements to serve higher-end chip segment in semiconductors
- Activated idle assets to serve need for domestic solar polysilicon
- Added capability to transform polysilicon into higher-value domestically made solar wafers
- Sold out polysilicon and wafer capacity in 2025; >80% capacity committed for next 5 years
- Built largest solar ingot and wafer facility in United States, co-located with polysilicon facility in Hemlock, Michigan
- Factory contains as much steel as Salesforce Tower; site equivalent of 60 football fields
- In Q3, moving from producing thousands of wafers a day to more than a million a day
- Q1 2025: $200 million sales run rate
- Expect to triple that run rate by 2027, adding $1.6 billion of new annualized revenue
- Goal: build $2.5 billion revenue stream by end of 2028
