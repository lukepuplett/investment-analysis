# Corning Inc. (GLW) – Financial Analysis
**Date:** January 28, 2026 | **Fiscal Period:** FY2025 (Year-end 12/31/2025)

---

## Executive Summary

**BLUF: Corning delivered outstanding FY2025 results with $16.4B revenue (+13% YoY), net income of $2.8B (+31% YoY), and operating margins of 18.59%, achieving its 20% margin target one year early. The company demonstrated strong financial discipline with $1.7B free cash flow and delivered both operating margin and sales targets ahead of schedule, positioning it well for continued growth under the upgraded Springboard plan.**

---

## Key Financial Metrics

### Income Statement Performance
- **Revenue:** $16.4B (+13% YoY from $14.5B)
  - Q4 2025: $4.4B (+14% YoY), indicating accelerating momentum
  - Full-year growth acceleration from 8% in prior periods

- **Operating Income:** $3.0B (+26% YoY)
  - Operating margin: 18.59% (+188 bps YoY)
  - Achieved 20% operating margin target one year ahead of original 2026 timeline

- **Net Income:** $2.8B (+31% YoY from $2.2B in 2024)
  - Net margin: 17.20% (+234 bps YoY)
  - Demonstrates operating leverage as margins expand faster than revenue growth

- **EPS:** $2.52 (+22% YoY)
  - Diluted EPS growing faster than net income due to aggressive buyback program
  - 2026E guidance: $0.66-0.70 for Q1 (+26% YoY)

### Cash Flow Strength
- **Operating Cash Flow:** $2.5B (+43% YoY)
  - OCF as % of revenue: 15.2%, up from 12.0% in 2024
  - Demonstrates improving cash generation capabilities

- **Free Cash Flow:** $1.4B (+62% YoY from $873M in 2024)
  - FCF margin: 8.6% of revenue
  - FCF conversion: 50% of net income (healthy for capital-intensive business)

- **Capital Expenditures:** $283M (1.7% of revenue)
  - Down from 1.8% in 2024, reflecting shift toward asset-light model
  - CapEx/D&A ratio of 14.7% indicates maintenance-level investment
  - Indicates capacity investments are being funded through customer prepayments

### Balance Sheet Health
- **Total Assets:** $29.4B (+6% YoY)
  - Asset turnover: 0.56x (improving efficiency)

- **Total Debt:** $8.0B (up from $5.1B)
  - Debt-to-Equity: 0.75x (moderate leverage)
  - Net Debt: $6.4B (reflecting strategic debt increases for growth investments)
  - Average debt maturity: 21 years (among longest in S&P 500)

- **Cash Position:** $1.6B
  - Slight decline from $1.8B reflects dividend payments and capital allocation
  - Sufficient liquidity for operational needs

- **Shareholders' Equity:** $10.7B (+4% YoY)
  - ROE: 26.3% (excellent capital efficiency)
  - ROA: 9.6% (strong asset utilization)

## Segment Performance

### Optical Communications (35% of revenue, 32% of net income)
- **Full Year 2025:**
  - Revenue: $6.3B (+35% YoY) — highest growth rate
  - Net income: $1.0B (+71% YoY)
  - Net margin: 16% (vs 14% prior year)

- **Growth Drivers:**
  - Enterprise/data center business: +61% YoY
  - Hyperscale data center portion: "significantly faster" growth (implies >61%)
  - New Gen AI product adoption driving demand
  - Meta partnership represents key validation

- **Assessment:** This segment is the growth engine, benefiting from AI infrastructure buildout. The 35% YoY growth rate is exceptional for a segment at this scale.

### Display (20% of revenue, 29% of net income)
- **Full Year 2025:**
  - Net income: $993M (up from target range of $900-950M)
  - Net margin: 27% (exceeding 25% target)

- **2026 Outlook:**
  - Q1: Mid-single digit sequential decline (normal seasonality)
  - Maintained annual guidance: $900-950M net income at 25% margin
  - Yen hedging in place through 2030 (mitigates currency risk)

- **Assessment:** Stable, profitable segment providing cash generation. Double-digit price increases implemented H2 2024 supporting margin maintenance despite weaker yen.

### Specialty Materials (13% of revenue, 13% of net income)
- **Full Year 2025:**
  - Revenue: $2.2B (+10% YoY)
  - Net income: $367M (+41% YoY)
  - Net margin: 16.7% (expanding from 15%)

- **Growth Drivers:**
  - Premium product demand strength
  - Gorilla Glass Solutions business acceleration
  - Flagship device adoption

- **2026 Outlook:**
  - "Significant growth" expected as part of upgraded Springboard plan
  - Expanded Apple partnership creating larger, longer-term growth driver
  - Samsung Galaxy Z Trifold adoption (ultrathin bendable glass)

- **Assessment:** Strong margin expansion and premium positioning. Foldable phone market represents significant TAM expansion opportunity.

### Automotive (8% of revenue, 2% of net income)
- **Full Year 2025:**
  - Revenue down 3% YoY
  - Net income: $63M (+3% YoY)
  - Challenged by heavy-duty diesel market weakness

- **2026 Outlook:**
  - Light-duty vehicle production: flat to slightly down
  - Heavy-duty market: expected to remain flat
  - Waiting for emissions regulation-driven content increase

- **Assessment:** Headwind segment in near term. Long-term opportunity from emissions compliance requirements and technical glass/optics adoption, but timing uncertain.

### Hemlock/Emerging Growth (9% of revenue)
- **Full Year 2025 Q4:**
  - Revenue: $526M (+62% YoY)
  - Net income: $1M (down YoY due to ramp costs)

- **2026 Outlook:**
  - Building $2.5B revenue stream by 2028
  - Current profitability drag of $0.03-0.05 per share in Q1 2026
  - Q1 guidance includes continued temporary impact from solar ramp

- **Assessment:** Significant investment phase. Polysilicon/solar ramp is strategically important for 2028 targets but pressuring near-term profitability.

## Profitability Analysis

### Margin Expansion Drivers
1. **Operating Leverage:** Revenue growth of 13% driving 26% operating income growth
2. **Mix Shift:** Higher-margin Optical Communications (16% margin) growing 35% YoY
3. **Efficiency:** Operating expenses as % of revenue declining as company scales
4. **Pricing Power:** Successful price increases in Display (yen hedging strategy)

### Margin Sustainability
- Company maintaining 20%+ operating margin target despite 13% revenue growth
- Indicates confidence in expanding margins further as growth continues
- Historical trend: margins expanding from 11.6% (2022) → 18.6% (2025)

## Key Concerns & Watchpoints

4. **Customer Concentration:** Hyperscaler/Meta concentration risk in Optical segment

## Outlook & Guidance

**Q1 2026:**
- Revenue: $4.2-4.3B (+15% YoY)
- EPS: $0.66-0.70 (+26% YoY)
- Solar ramp impact: $0.03-0.05/share temporary headwind

**Full Year 2026:**
- CapEx: ~$1.7B (few hundred million above depreciation)
- Profitability expected to improve through year as solar drag eases
- Operating margin target: 20%+ maintained

**Springboard Plan 2026-2028:**
- 2026 high confidence: $5.75B incremental sales (up from $4.0B)
- 2028 internal plan: $11B incremental sales (up from $8B)
- Target annualized sales: $24B by end-2028 (from $13.1B at Springboard launch)
- Operating margin: 20%+ sustained throughout growth

## Conclusion

Corning has demonstrated exceptional financial execution in FY2025, with strong revenue growth accelerating into 2026, margins expanding ahead of schedule, and cash generation significantly improving. The company's ability to grow revenue 13% while expanding operating margins 188 bps reflects the quality of the Optical Communications growth and the operational leverage of the Springboard strategy. The upgraded plan targets indicate management confidence in maintaining this trajectory through 2028. Key monitoring points are automotive recovery timing, solar profitability inflection, and sustained Optical Communications growth.
