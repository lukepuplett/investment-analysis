### Synthesis Highlights
- **Execution Watchpoints:** Capacity tightness and solar ramp remain the unresolved red flags; track optical backlog-to-capacity and solar yields quarterly.
- **Moat & Thesis:** Materials science IP, customer intimacy, and margin discipline keep the thesis intact; solar execution is the lone ⚠️ pillar.
- **Valuation Focus:** Revenue trajectory and discount rate assumptions swing value the most (~$15/share delta each), underscoring sensitivity to growth credibility and macro rates.
- **Next Steps:** Update tables post Q4 print—validate solar yields, check margin cadence, and revisit valuation sensitivities as new guidance arrives.
