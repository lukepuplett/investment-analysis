**Document Date:** 02/23/2025

**Analysis Date:** 02/23/2025

---

## Executive Summary (BLUF)

**Microsoft delivered Q2 FY25 revenue of $69.6B (+12% YoY) and TTM revenue of $281.7B (+15% YoY) with industry-leading profitability: operating margin 46.7%, net margin 38.1%, and FCF of $84.5B TTM. Microsoft Cloud exceeded $40B in quarterly revenue (+21% YoY) and AI revenue run rate exceeded $13B (+175% YoY). Balance sheet is strong with ~$90B cash and short-term investments and modest leverage (total debt ~$62B). Capital returns totaled $9.7B in the quarter (dividends and buybacks). Key financial dynamic: Azure and AI driving growth and margin expansion while Productivity and MPC provide stable cash generation. Main risks: capital intensity for AI/datacenter buildout and dependency on enterprise spend.**
