**Analysis Date:** 02/23/2025

## Executive Summary (BLUF)

**Microsoft is a core holding in cloud and enterprise software with TTM revenue of $281.7B, industry-leading margins (operating 46.7%, net 38.1%), and FCF of $84.5B. Thesis: Azure and AI (run rate >$13B, +175% YoY) drive growth and margin expansion while Productivity (M365, Dynamics) and the installed base provide stability and cash flow. Risks: cloud competition (AWS, GCP), regulatory scrutiny, and AI CapEx intensity. Recommendation: **Hold/Buy** for long-term investors; valuation full but supported by quality and growth. Probability-weighted view: Base Case ~$420–450; Bull Case upside to $500+ on sustained Azure and AI outperformance.**
