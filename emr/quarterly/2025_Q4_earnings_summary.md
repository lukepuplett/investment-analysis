## Analysis

### Q4 2025 Results

**Mixed Results:**
- ✅ **Revenue Beat:** Net sales of $6.44B exceeded estimates by $110M (+1.7%)
- ❌ **EPS Miss:** Adj EPS of $1.40 missed by $0.21 (-13.0%) - significant miss
- ❌ **Underlying Growth Miss:** +4.0% vs. +5.41% expected (-141 bps)

### 2026 Guidance - Significant Disappointment

**2026 Underlying Sales Growth: ~+2%**
- **Expected:** +5.18% 
- **Variance:** -318 basis points
- **Interpretation:** Guidance significantly below expectations, indicating:
  - Slower recovery in discrete markets than expected
  - Continued headwinds in Europe/China
  - Power cycle moderation
  - Overall industrial automation slowdown

### Key Takeaways

**Negative:**
1. **EPS Miss:** -13% miss on EPS despite revenue beat suggests margin pressure
2. **Growth Guidance:** +2% for 2026 is well below expectations (+5.18%)
3. **Discrete Recovery:** Slower than expected discrete market recovery
4. **Margin Compression:** Revenue beat but EPS miss indicates margin challenges

**Positive:**
1. **Revenue Beat:** Still generating revenue growth (+4% underlying)
2. **Market Position:** Strong backlog and market positions remain intact
3. **Long-term Thesis:** Structural trends (power generation, automation) remain intact
