## Critical Accounting Estimates & Changes

### Change in Depreciation Estimate (Effective Jan 1, 2025)
- **Change:** Useful life of servers/networking equipment reduced from 6 years to 5 years
- **Reason:** Increased pace of technology development, particularly in AI/ML
- **Q3 2025 Impact:** +$392M depreciation expense, -$298M net income (-$0.03 EPS)
- **9M 2025 Impact:** +$889M depreciation expense, -$677M net income (-$0.06 EPS)
- **Primary Impact:** AWS segment

### Revenue Recognition - Unearned Revenue
- **Unearned Revenue (Sept 30, 2025):** $21.1B (down from $24.6B at Dec 31, 2024)
- **Primary Source:** AWS prepayments and Prime memberships
- **Performance Obligations:** ~$200B in AWS commitments not yet recognized
- **Weighted-average remaining contract life:** 3.8 years

## Key Risk Factors (Updated Q3 2025)

### Business & Industry Risks
1. Intense competition across all business segments
2. Expansion into new products/services/geographies
3. International operations exposure
4. Retail demand variability and seasonality
5. Third-party seller fraud and unlawful activities
6. Intellectual property protection and infringement claims

### Operational Risks
1. Fulfillment network optimization challenges
2. Data loss and security incidents
3. System interruptions and lack of redundancy
4. Key personnel retention
5. Supplier relationship dependencies
6. Commercial agreement/alliance complexities

### Financial Risks
1. Foreign exchange volatility
2. Inventory management challenges
3. Payments-related risks
4. Equity investment volatility (especially Anthropic/Rivian)

### Legal & Regulatory Risks
1. Government regulation evolution (AI, data privacy, competition)
2. FTC and antitrust investigations ongoing
3. Product liability claims
4. Tax liabilities across jurisdictions
5. Government contract compliance requirements

## Summary

Amazon's Q3 2025 10-Q shows strong business momentum with:
- **13% revenue growth** on massive scale ($503.5B YTD)
- **AWS acceleration to 20%** growth (highest in 11 quarters)
- **Heavy capex investment** ($120B+) in AI infrastructure
- **Solid profitability** despite significant infrastructure investments
- **Strong balance sheet** with positive net cash position
- **Ongoing regulatory/legal challenges** (FTC settlement, antitrust cases)

The company is in a significant investment cycle prioritizing AI infrastructure and scale over near-term margin optimization.
